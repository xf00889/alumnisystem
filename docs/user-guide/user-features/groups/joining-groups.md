# Joining Groups

Learn how to join alumni groups, answer security questions for verification, and manage your group memberships.

## Overview

Joining alumni groups allows you to connect with fellow alumni, participate in discussions, attend group events, and access shared resources. The joining process varies depending on the group's settings - some groups allow instant joining while others require approval or answering security questions.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **Required**: Authenticated alumni account
- **Access Level**: All registered alumni users
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Verified email address
  - Completed profile setup

## How to Access

1. Log in to your alumni account
2. Navigate to **"Groups"** in the main navigation
3. Find a group you want to join (see [Browsing Groups](browsing-groups.md))
4. Click on the group to view details
5. Click the **"Join"** or **"Join Group"** button

## Understanding Group Join Requirements

### Group Types and Join Process

Different groups have different joining requirements:

**Public Groups (No Approval Required):**
- Click "Join" and you're instantly a member
- No waiting period
- Immediate access to group content

**Public Groups (Approval Required):**
- Click "Join" to submit a request
- Wait for admin approval
- Receive notification when approved

**Restricted Groups (Security Questions):**
- Click "Join" to start the process
- Answer required security questions
- Wait for admin to review your answers
- Receive notification when approved or rejected

**Private Groups:**
- Not visible in public listings
- Invitation-only or direct link required
- May require approval and/or security questions

## Joining a Group (Simple Process)

### Step-by-Step: Join a Group Without Approval

1. Navigate to the Groups List page
2. Find the group you want to join
3. Click on the group card to view details
4. Review the group information:
   - Group description
   - Member count
   - Group type
   - Recent activity
5. Click the **"Join Group"** button
6. Confirmation message appears: "You have successfully joined the group"
7. You are now a member and can access group content

**Expected Result:**
- Your membership status changes to "Member"
- You can immediately view and post in the group
- The group appears in your "My Groups" list
- You receive notifications for group activities

### Step-by-Step: Join a Group With Approval

1. Navigate to the group detail page
2. Click the **"Join Group"** button
3. Confirmation message appears: "Your request to join has been submitted and is pending approval"
4. Your membership status shows "Pending"
5. Wait for group admin to review your request
6. Receive notification when approved

**Expected Result:**
- Your join request is submitted
- Status shows "Pending Approval"
- You cannot access group content yet
- You receive email notification when approved

**Waiting for Approval:**
- Check your notifications regularly
- Be patient - admins may take time to review
- You can view the group page but not the content
- A "Pending Approval" message is displayed

## Joining Groups with Security Questions

### Understanding Security Questions

Some groups require you to answer security questions to verify you're a legitimate member. This is common for:
- Batch-specific groups (to verify you're from that batch)
- Campus-specific groups (to verify your campus affiliation)
- Restricted membership groups

**Question Types:**
- Batch verification (e.g., "Who was your class adviser?")
- Campus verification (e.g., "Name a building on campus")
- Course verification (e.g., "Name a professor in your department")
- Personal verification (e.g., "Name a classmate from your batch")

### Step-by-Step: Join Group with Security Questions

1. Navigate to the group detail page
2. Click the **"Join Group"** button
3. You are redirected to the Security Questions page
4. Review the questions displayed
5. Answer each required question:
   - Required questions are marked with an asterisk (*)
   - Optional questions can be skipped
   - Provide honest and accurate answers
6. Review your answers for accuracy
7. Click **"Submit Answers"**
8. Confirmation message appears: "Your request to join has been submitted and is pending approval"

**Expected Result:**
- Your answers are submitted to group admins
- Your membership status shows "Pending"
- Admins review your answers before approval
- You receive notification of approval or rejection

### Tips for Answering Security Questions

**Be Accurate:**
- Provide truthful answers
- Don't guess if you're unsure
- Admins verify answers against known information

**Be Specific:**
- Give detailed answers when possible
- Avoid vague or generic responses
- Include relevant context if helpful

**Be Respectful:**
- Answer all required questions
- Take the process seriously
- Don't provide joke or inappropriate answers

**Common Questions:**
- "What year did you graduate?"
- "Who was your thesis adviser?"
- "Name three classmates from your batch"
- "What was your major subject?"
- "Name a memorable event from your batch"

## Viewing Group Details Before Joining

### Step-by-Step: Review Group Information

1. Click on a group card from the Groups List
2. View the Group Detail page showing:
   - **Group Name and Description**
   - **Group Type**: Automatic, Manual, or Hybrid
   - **Visibility**: Public, Private, or Restricted
   - **Member Count**: Number of active members
   - **Group Settings**:
     - Requires approval: Yes/No
     - Has security questions: Yes/No
     - Maximum members: Number or unlimited
   - **Recent Activities**: Latest group actions
   - **Upcoming Events**: Group events you can attend
   - **Group Files**: Shared resources (if visible)
3. Decide if you want to join based on this information
4. Click **"Join Group"** if interested

**Expected Result:**
- You have complete information about the group
- You understand the joining requirements
- You can make an informed decision
- You know what to expect after joining

## Managing Your Join Requests

### Viewing Pending Requests

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Go to the Groups List page
2. Look for groups with "Pending" status badge
3. Click on a pending group to view details
4. See the "Pending Approval" message
5. Wait for admin action

**What You Can See:**
- Groups where you have pending requests
- Request submission date
- Group information
- Estimated approval time (if provided)

**What You Cannot Do:**
- Access group content
- Post in the group
- View member-only information
- Participate in discussions

### Canceling a Join Request

If you change your mind about joining a group:

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Navigate to the group detail page
2. Click the **"Cancel Request"** button
3. Confirm cancellation in the popup
4. Your request is withdrawn

**Expected Result:**
- Your join request is removed
- Status returns to "Not a Member"
- You can rejoin later if you change your mind
- No notification is sent to admins

## After Joining a Group

### What Happens Next

**Immediate Access (No Approval Required):**
- You can view all group content
- You can post and comment
- You can access group files
- You can RSVP to events
- You receive group notifications

**After Approval (Approval Required):**
- You receive an email notification
- You receive an in-app notification
- You can now access all group content
- Your status changes from "Pending" to "Member"

### Your Member Dashboard

Once you're a member, you can:

**View Content:**
- Read all posts and discussions
- View group files and resources
- See member list
- Access group events

**Participate:**
- Create posts (if allowed)
- Comment on posts
- Like and react to content
- Upload files (if allowed)

**Engage:**
- RSVP to events
- Send messages to group
- Connect with other members
- Receive notifications

## Membership Status Types

### Understanding Status Indicators

**Not a Member:**
- You haven't joined the group
- "Join" button is visible
- You can view basic group info only

**Pending:**
- Your join request is awaiting approval
- "Pending Approval" message is displayed
- You cannot access group content yet

**Member:**
- Your membership is approved and active
- You have full access to group features
- "Member" badge is displayed

**Rejected:**
- Your join request was declined
- You can request to join again
- Consider contacting admin for clarification

**Blocked:**
- You have been removed from the group
- You cannot rejoin without admin action
- Contact admin if you believe this is an error

## Tips and Best Practices

### Before Joining
- Read the group description carefully
- Check the member count and activity level
- Review recent posts to understand group culture
- Verify it's the right group for you
- Check if security questions are required

### When Answering Security Questions
- Be honest and accurate
- Provide specific details
- Don't rush through questions
- Double-check your answers
- Contact admin if you have questions

### After Joining
- Introduce yourself to the group
- Read group rules and guidelines
- Review recent posts to understand context
- Participate respectfully
- Engage with other members

### Managing Memberships
- Join groups that interest you
- Don't join too many groups at once
- Leave groups you're not active in
- Respond to group notifications
- Participate regularly in groups you join

## Common Use Cases

### Joining Your Batch Group

**Scenario:** You want to connect with your graduation year classmates

**Steps:**
1. Search for your batch year group
2. Verify it matches your course and campus
3. Click "Join Group"
4. Answer security questions about your batch
5. Wait for approval from batch admin
6. Start connecting with classmates

### Joining an Interest Group

**Scenario:** You want to join a professional or hobby group

**Steps:**
1. Search for groups related to your interest
2. Review group description and activity
3. Click "Join Group"
4. If approval required, wait for admin review
5. Introduce yourself after joining
6. Participate in discussions

### Joining Multiple Groups

**Scenario:** You want to be part of several groups

**Steps:**
1. Browse and identify relevant groups
2. Join groups one at a time
3. Complete any required security questions
4. Wait for approvals if needed
5. Manage notifications for multiple groups
6. Participate actively in each group

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Join Group

**Problem:** "Join" button is not working

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check if you're already a member
- Verify your account is verified
- Check if group has reached maximum members
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Contact support if issue persists

### Join Request Not Submitted

**Problem:** Request doesn't seem to go through

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify all required fields are completed
- Answer all required security questions
- Look for error messages on the form
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if needed

### Security Questions Not Appearing

**Problem:** Expected security questions but none shown

**Solutions:**
- Group may not have security questions enabled
- Questions may be optional
- Refresh the page
- Contact group admin for clarification
- Proceed with regular join process

### Join Request Rejected

**Problem:** Your request was declined

**Solutions:**
- Review rejection reason if provided
- Contact group admin for explanation
- Verify you answered questions correctly
- Check if you meet group requirements
- Request to join again with correct information
- Consider joining a different group

### Pending Request Taking Too Long

**Problem:** Waiting a long time for approval

**Solutions:**
- Be patient - admins may be busy
- Check if admin is active (recent group activity)
- Send a polite message to group admin
- Verify your request was actually submitted
- Consider joining other groups while waiting
- Contact support if excessively delayed

## Leaving a Group

### When to Leave a Group

Consider leaving a group if:
- You're no longer interested in the group's purpose
- The group is inactive
- You're in too many groups
- The group doesn't meet your expectations
- You're graduating or changing careers

### Step-by-Step: Leave a Group

1. Navigate to the group detail page
2. Click the **"Leave Group"** button (usually in group settings)
3. A confirmation dialog appears
4. Optionally provide a reason for leaving
5. Click **"Confirm Leave"**
6. Confirmation message: "You have successfully left the group"

**Expected Result:**
- Your membership is removed
- You lose access to group content
- The group is removed from "My Groups"
- You can rejoin later if you change your mind

**Important Notes:**
- Group admins cannot leave if they're the only admin
- Leaving is permanent - you'll need to rejoin to access content
- Your previous posts remain in the group
- You stop receiving group notifications

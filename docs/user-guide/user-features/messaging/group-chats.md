# Group Chats

This guide explains how to use group messaging within Alumni Groups in the NORSU Alumni System.

## Overview

Group chats allow members of an alumni group to communicate with all other group members simultaneously. This feature is integrated into the Alumni Groups system and provides a space for group discussions, announcements, and collaboration.

## Who Can Use Group Chats

- **Required**: Authenticated alumni account
- **Access Level**: Approved group members only
- **Prerequisites**:
  - Verified email address
  - Membership in at least one alumni group
  - Approved membership status in the group

## Understanding Group Messaging

### What is Group Messaging?

Group messaging is a feature within Alumni Groups that allows all approved members to:
- Send messages visible to all group members
- Participate in group-wide discussions
- Share information with the entire group
- Collaborate on group activities

### Difference from Direct Messaging

| Feature | Direct Messaging | Group Messaging |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Participants** | Two people (one-on-one) | All group members |
| **Privacy** | Private between two users | Visible to all group members |
| **Access** | Requires connection | Requires group membership |
| **Purpose** | Personal communication | Group collaboration |
| **Location** | Messaging page | Within group page |

## Accessing Group Chats

### Method 1: From Group Page

#### Step 1: Navigate to Your Group

1. Log in to your NORSU Alumni account
2. Click on **"Groups"** or **"Alumni Groups"** in the main navigation
3. Find the group you want to message in
4. Click on the group name or **"View Group"** button

**Expected Result**: The group detail page opens, showing group information and activities.

![Group Detail Page](../../screenshots/group-detail-page.png)

#### Step 2: Access Group Chat

1. On the group detail page, look for the **"Messages"** or **"Group Chat"** tab/section
2. Click on the messages section
3. The group chat interface loads

**Expected Result**: You see the group chat area with previous messages and an input field to send new messages.

![Group Chat Section](../../screenshots/group-chat-section.png)

### Method 2: From Groups List

#### Step 1: View Your Groups

1. Navigate to the Alumni Groups page
2. Look for **"My Groups"** section or filter
3. This shows all groups you're a member of

#### Step 2: Select a Group

1. Click on a group card
2. Navigate to the messages/chat section within the group

**Expected Result**: The group chat opens, ready for messaging.

## Sending Messages in Group Chats

### Sending Text Messages

#### Step 1: Open the Group Chat

1. Navigate to the group's chat section (as described above)
2. Ensure you're viewing the correct group
3. The message input area appears at the bottom

**Expected Result**: You see the chat history and an input field labeled "Type your message..." or similar.

![Group Chat Input](../../screenshots/group-chat-input.png)

#### Step 2: Type Your Message

1. Click in the message input field
2. Type your message content
3. Keep messages relevant to the group's purpose

**Message Guidelines**:
- Keep messages on-topic for the group
- Be respectful to all group members
- Follow group rules and guidelines
- Use clear, professional language
- Consider that all members will see your message

#### Step 3: Send the Message

1. After typing your message, click the **"Send"** button
2. Or press **Enter** on your keyboard (if enabled)

**Expected Result**:
- Your message appears in the group chat immediately
- All group members can see your message
- Your name and profile photo appear with the message
- The timestamp shows when you sent it
- The message input field clears

![Group Message Sent](../../screenshots/group-message-sent.png)

### Message Display

Group messages show:
- **Sender's Name**: Who sent the message
- **Profile Photo**: Sender's profile picture
- **Message Content**: The text of the message
- **Timestamp**: When the message was sent
- **Your Messages**: May be highlighted or positioned differently

### Group Chat Features

Depending on the system configuration, group chats may include:
- **Message History**: View all past group messages
- **Member List**: See who's in the group
- **Notifications**: Get notified of new group messages
- **Search**: Find specific messages (if available)
- **Reactions**: React to messages (if available)

## Viewing Group Chat History

### Scrolling Through Messages

#### Viewing Recent Messages

1. When you open the group chat, recent messages appear
2. Scroll up to see older messages
3. Messages load as you scroll

**Expected Result**: The complete group chat history is accessible.

### Message Organization

Group messages are organized:
- **Chronologically**: Oldest to newest (or newest to oldest)
- **By Date**: Date separators show when messages were sent
- **By Sender**: Each message shows who sent it
- **With Timestamps**: Exact time for each message

![Group Chat History](../../screenshots/group-chat-history.png)

### Finding Specific Messages

**Scroll Method**:
1. Scroll through the chat manually
2. Look for date separators
3. Identify messages by sender name

**Search Method** (if available):
1. Look for a search icon or field
2. Enter keywords to find specific messages
3. Results highlight matching messages

## Managing Group Chat Participation

### Who Can See Group Messages

- **Approved Members Only**: Only users with approved membership status
- **Not Visible to Non-Members**: People outside the group cannot see messages
- **Not Visible to Pending Members**: Users awaiting approval cannot see messages
- **Visible to All Approved Members**: All approved members see all messages

### Who Can Send Messages

Typically, all approved group members can send messages, but this may depend on:
- **Group Settings**: Admins may restrict who can post
- **Member Role**: Some groups may limit messaging to certain roles
- **Group Type**: Different group types may have different rules
- **Moderation**: Some groups may require message approval

### Message Moderation

Some groups may have message moderation:
- **Pre-Moderation**: Messages require approval before appearing
- **Post-Moderation**: Messages appear immediately but can be removed
- **Admin Review**: Admins can delete inappropriate messages
- **Reporting**: Members may be able to report inappropriate messages

## Group Chat Notifications

### Receiving Notifications

When someone sends a group message:
- **In-App Notification**: Bell icon shows new notification
- **Email Notification**: You may receive an email (based on settings)
- **Group Badge**: Unread message count on group card
- **Browser Notification**: Desktop notification (if enabled)

### Managing Notifications

To control group chat notifications:
1. Go to your account settings or group settings
2. Look for notification preferences
3. Choose notification options:
   - All group messages
   - Only mentions (if available)
   - Important messages only
   - No notifications

**Note**: Notification options may vary by system configuration.

## Group Chat Etiquette

### Best Practices

1. **Stay On-Topic**
   - Keep messages relevant to the group's purpose
   - Use appropriate channels if available
   - Don't spam the group with off-topic content

2. **Be Respectful**
   - Treat all members with respect
   - Avoid offensive language
   - Don't attack or harass other members
   - Respect different opinions

3. **Be Considerate**
   - Don't send too many messages at once
   - Avoid sending messages at inappropriate times
   - Use @mentions sparingly (if available)
   - Don't flood the chat

4. **Follow Group Rules**
   - Read and follow group guidelines
   - Respect admin decisions
   - Report violations appropriately
   - Help maintain a positive environment

### What to Share

**Appropriate Content**:
- Group-related announcements
- Event information
- Questions for the group
- Relevant resources and links
- Congratulations and celebrations
- Group collaboration discussions

**Avoid Sharing**:
- Spam or promotional content
- Inappropriate or offensive material
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Confidential information
- Excessive personal messages
- Off-topic discussions

## Creating and Managing Groups

### Creating a Group with Chat

To create a new alumni group with chat functionality:

1. **Navigate to Groups**: Go to Alumni Groups page
2. **Create Group**: Click **"Create Group"** button
3. **Fill Details**: Provide group information
4. **Configure Settings**: Set group type and visibility
5. **Submit**: Create the group

**Expected Result**: A new group is created with chat functionality enabled for approved members.

For detailed instructions, see [Alumni Groups Documentation](../groups/README.md).

### Group Admin Controls

If you're a group admin, you may have additional controls:
- **Moderate Messages**: Review and remove inappropriate messages
- **Manage Members**: Add or remove group members
- **Configure Settings**: Enable/disable chat features
- **Set Permissions**: Control who can send messages
- **Pin Messages**: Highlight important messages (if available)

## Adding Participants

### How Members Join Groups

Group membership is managed at the group level, not within the chat:

1. **Join Group**: Users request to join the group
2. **Approval**: Admins approve membership (if required)
3. **Access Chat**: Approved members can access group chat
4. **Automatic Access**: All approved members have chat access

**Note**: You cannot add individual participants to group chat - they must join the group.

### Inviting Members to Groups

To invite alumni to join your group:

1. **Share Group Link**: Share the group page URL
2. **Direct Invitation**: Tell them the group name to search for
3. **Admin Invitation**: Admins may have invite features (if available)

For detailed instructions, see [Alumni Groups - Joining Groups](../groups/joining-groups.md).

## Removing Participants

### Leaving a Group

To leave a group and its chat:

1. Navigate to the group page
2. Look for **"Leave Group"** button
3. Confirm you want to leave
4. You'll lose access to the group chat

**Expected Result**: You're removed from the group and can no longer see or send messages.

**Note**: If you're the only admin, you may need to assign another admin before leaving.

### Removing Members (Admin Only)

Group admins can remove members:

1. Go to group management page
2. Find the member to remove
3. Click **"Remove"** or **"Block"**
4. Confirm the action

**Expected Result**: The member loses access to the group and its chat.

## Group Chat Settings

### Available Settings (Admin)

Group admins may be able to configure:
- **Message Approval**: Require approval for messages
- **Who Can Post**: Restrict posting to certain roles
- **Chat History**: Control message retention
- **Notifications**: Set default notification settings
- **Moderation**: Enable/disable moderation features

### Accessing Settings

1. Navigate to the group page
2. Look for **"Settings"** or **"Manage Group"**
3. Find chat or messaging settings
4. Make desired changes
5. Save settings

**Note**: Only group admins can access these settings.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot See Group Chat

**Problem**: Group chat section is not visible

**Possible Causes**:
- Not a member of the group
- Membership not approved yet
- Group chat disabled for this group
- Permission issues

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're a member of the group
2. Check your membership status (should be "Approved")
3. Contact group admin if you should have access
4. Refresh the page
5. Try a different browser

### Cannot Send Messages

**Problem**: Unable to send messages in group chat

**Possible Causes**:
- Membership not approved
- Posting restricted by group settings
- Account restrictions
- Technical issues

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your membership is approved
2. Check if you have posting permissions
3. Ensure your account is active
4. Refresh the page
5. Clear browser cache
6. Contact group admin

### Messages Not Appearing

**Problem**: Sent messages don't appear in chat

**Possible Causes**:
- Message requires approval
- Internet connection issues
- Browser issues
- System delay

**Solutions**:
1. Wait a few seconds for processing
2. Check if message approval is required
3. Refresh the page
4. Check internet connection
5. Try sending again
6. Contact support if issue persists

### Cannot Find Group Chat

**Problem**: Can't locate the chat section in a group

**Solutions**:
1. Look for "Messages", "Chat", or "Discussion" tabs
2. Scroll down the group page
3. Check if chat is enabled for this group
4. Ask group admin where chat is located
5. Refer to group documentation

### Notifications Not Working

**Problem**: Not receiving group chat notifications

**Solutions**:
1. Check notification settings in your account
2. Verify group notification preferences
3. Enable browser notifications
4. Check email notification settings
5. Ensure notifications aren't muted for this group

## Privacy and Security

### Message Privacy

- **Group Members Only**: Only approved members see messages
- **No Public Access**: Messages not visible outside the group
- **Admin Visibility**: Group admins can see all messages
- **Permanent Record**: Messages may be stored permanently

### Protecting Your Privacy

1. **Be Careful What You Share**
   - Don't share sensitive personal information
   - Avoid sharing private contact details
   - Be cautious with confidential information
   - Remember all members can see messages

2. **Report Inappropriate Content**
   - Report spam or harassment
   - Contact group admins
   - Use reporting features if available
   - Document serious violations

3. **Understand Group Visibility**
   - Know who can see your messages
   - Check group privacy settings
   - Be aware of group type (public/private)
   - Consider message permanence

### Security Best Practices

- Don't share passwords or sensitive data
- Be wary of suspicious links
- Verify information before acting on it
- Report security concerns to admins
- Keep your account secure

## Limitations and Restrictions

### Group Chat Limits

- **Member-Only Access**: Must be approved group member
- **No Private Messages**: All messages visible to group
- **Admin Moderation**: Admins may moderate content
- **Group Rules**: Must follow group guidelines
- **Message Limits**: May have character or frequency limits

### What You Cannot Do

- **Cannot Create Private Chats**: Group chat is for all members
- **Cannot Delete Others' Messages**: Only admins can moderate
- **Cannot Remove Yourself from Notifications**: Must leave group
- **Cannot Control Who Sees Messages**: All approved members see all messages

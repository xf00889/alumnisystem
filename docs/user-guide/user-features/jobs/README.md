# Jobs

The Job Board is a comprehensive career platform that connects NORSU alumni with employment opportunities. Whether you're looking for your next career move or exploring new opportunities, the job board provides powerful search, filtering, and skill-matching capabilities to help you find the right position.

## Available Features

### For All Users

- **[Browsing Jobs](browsing-jobs.md)** - Discover job opportunities, search and filter listings, and explore skill-based job matching
- **[Job Applications](job-applications.md)** - Apply for internal positions, track your application status, and manage your job search

### Key Capabilities

- Browse active job postings from NORSU and external organizations
- Search jobs by title, company, location, or description
- Filter by job type, source, and category
- View skill-based job matches based on your profile
- Apply for internal NORSU positions through the system
- Track your application status and history
- Access external job links for third-party opportunities

## Getting Started

1. **Update Your Profile** - Ensure your skills, education, and work experience are up to date for better job matching
2. **Browse Available Jobs** - Explore the job board to see current opportunities
3. **Use Skill Matching** - Enable skill-based view to see jobs that match your qualifications
4. **Apply for Positions** - Submit applications for internal jobs or follow links for external opportunities

## Job Types

The job board features two types of positions:

- **NORSU Internal Jobs**: Positions within NORSU or the alumni network that accept applications through the system
- **External Jobs**: Opportunities from external organizations with links to their application pages

# Making Donations

Learn how to make donations to campaigns, complete payment, upload proof, and track your donation history in the NORSU Alumni System.

## Overview

The donation process allows you to support fundraising campaigns through GCash payments. Whether you're a registered alumni member or a public visitor, you can contribute to causes that matter to you. The system guides you through the entire process from selecting an amount to receiving confirmation.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **Registered Alumni**: Full donation features with history tracking
- **Public Users**: Can donate to public campaigns (limited features)

## Prerequisites

Before making a donation, ensure you have:

- A GCash account with sufficient balance
- The campaign you want to support is accepting donations
- Valid email address (for confirmation and receipts)

## The Donation Process

The donation process consists of four main steps:

1. **Fill Donation Form** - Enter donation details
2. **View Payment Instructions** - Get GCash payment details
3. **Make Payment** - Complete payment via GCash
4. **Upload Proof** - Submit payment confirmation

## Step 1: Accessing the Donation Form

### For Registered Alumni

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **Donations** → **Browse Campaigns**
3. Find the campaign you want to support
4. Click on the campaign card to view details
5. Scroll to the **Make a Donation** section
6. The donation form will be displayed

### For Public Users

1. Visit the NORSU Alumni System website
2. Navigate to **Donations** or **Campaigns**
3. Browse public campaigns
4. Click on a campaign to view details
5. The donation form appears at the bottom of the page

## Step 2: Filling Out the Donation Form

### Required Information

**For Registered Alumni:**
- **Amount**: Enter the donation amount in Philippine Pesos (₱)
- **Message** (Optional): Add a personal message or dedication
- **Anonymous Donation** (Optional): Check to hide your name from public donor lists

**For Public Users (Non-Registered):**
- **Amount**: Enter the donation amount in Philippine Pesos (₱)
- **Your Name**: Enter your full name (required unless anonymous)
- **Your Email**: Enter your email address (required for confirmation)
- **Message** (Optional): Add a personal message or dedication
- **Anonymous Donation** (Optional): Check to hide your name from public donor lists

### Form Fields Explained

#### Amount
- Enter any amount you wish to donate
- Minimum amount: ₱1.00
- Use decimal format for centavos (e.g., 500.50)
- No maximum limit

#### Anonymous Donation
- **Checked**: Your name will not appear in public donor lists
- **Unchecked**: Your name will be visible to other users
- Your donation still counts toward campaign totals
- Administrators can still see your information

#### Message
- Optional personal message or dedication
- Maximum length varies by system configuration
- Visible to campaign administrators
- May be displayed publicly (unless anonymous)

### Completing the Form

1. Fill in all required fields
2. Review your information for accuracy
3. Ensure your email address is correct (you'll receive confirmation here)
4. Click the **Proceed to Payment** or **Donate Now** button
5. You'll be redirected to the payment instructions page

## Step 3: Payment Instructions

After submitting the donation form, you'll see the GCash payment instructions page.

### What You'll See

- **Donation Summary**: Your donation details and reference information
- **GCash QR Code**: Scan this with your GCash app
- **Account Details**: GCash number and account name
- **Payment Amount**: Exact amount to send
- **Payment Instructions**: Step-by-step guide

### GCash Payment Instructions

1. **Open GCash App**
   - Launch the GCash mobile application
   - Ensure you're logged in

2. **Scan QR Code**
   - Tap **Send Money** or **Pay QR**
   - Point your camera at the QR code displayed
   - Or manually enter the GCash number shown

3. **Enter Amount**
   - Enter the exact donation amount
   - Double-check the amount matches your donation
   - Do not add extra amounts without noting them

4. **Add Reference** (Optional but Recommended)
   - In the message/reference field, add your name or donation ID
   - This helps with verification

5. **Complete Payment**
   - Review all details carefully
   - Confirm the transaction
   - Wait for GCash confirmation

6. **Save Receipt**
   - Take a screenshot of the GCash confirmation screen
   - Make sure the reference number is visible
   - Keep this for your records

### Important Payment Notes

- **Pay Exact Amount**: Send exactly the amount shown
- **Don't Close Page**: Keep the payment instructions page open
- **Save Reference Number**: You'll need the GCash transaction reference number
- **Screenshot Required**: You must upload proof of payment

## Step 4: Uploading Payment Proof

After completing your GCash payment, you must upload proof to complete the donation.

### What You Need

- Screenshot of your GCash payment confirmation
- GCash transaction reference number (from your receipt)

### Upload Process

1. **Return to Payment Instructions Page**
   - If you closed it, check your email for the link
   - Or navigate to your donation history

2. **Locate Upload Section**
   - Scroll to the **Upload Payment Proof** section
   - You'll see a file upload field and reference number field

3. **Enter Reference Number**
   - Find the reference number on your GCash receipt
   - Enter it exactly as shown (alphanumeric)
   - This is **required** for verification

4. **Upload Screenshot**
   - Click **Choose File** or **Browse**
   - Select your GCash receipt screenshot
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
   - Maximum file size: 5MB

5. **Submit Proof**
   - Review your reference number for accuracy
   - Ensure the screenshot is clear and readable
   - Click **Submit Payment Proof** or **Complete Donation**

### Screenshot Requirements

Your payment proof screenshot must show:

- ✓ Transaction amount
- ✓ Transaction date and time
- ✓ Reference number
- ✓ Recipient name/number
- ✓ "Successful" or "Completed" status

**Tips for Good Screenshots:**
- Capture the entire confirmation screen
- Ensure text is readable and not blurry
- Don't crop out important information
- Use good lighting if photographing a screen

### After Submission

Once you submit your payment proof:

1. **Status Changes**: Your donation status changes to "Pending Verification"
2. **Email Confirmation**: You receive an email confirming submission
3. **Admin Review**: Administrators will verify your payment
4. **Final Confirmation**: You'll receive another email once verified

## Donation Status Tracking

### Donation Statuses

- **Pending Payment**: Waiting for you to complete GCash payment
- **Pending Verification**: Payment proof submitted, awaiting admin review
- **Completed**: Payment verified and donation processed
- **Failed**: Payment could not be verified or was rejected
- **Disputed**: Issue with payment requiring resolution

### Checking Your Donation Status

**For Registered Alumni:**

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **Donations** → **My Donation History**
3. Find your donation in the list
4. Status is displayed next to each donation
5. Click on a donation to see full details

**For Public Users:**

- Check your email for status updates
- Use the confirmation link sent to your email
- Contact administrators if you don't receive updates

## Viewing Donation History

### Accessing Your History (Registered Alumni Only)

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **Donations** in the main menu
3. Select **My Donation History** or **Donation History**
4. View all your past donations

### What You'll See

- **Donation List**: All your donations in chronological order
- **Campaign Names**: Which campaigns you supported
- **Amounts**: How much you donated to each
- **Dates**: When donations were made
- **Status**: Current status of each donation
- **Reference Numbers**: Transaction references
- **Receipts**: Download or view receipts (if available)

### Donation History Statistics

Your donation history page may show:

- **Total Donated**: Sum of all completed donations
- **Campaigns Supported**: Number of different campaigns
- **Recent Activity**: Your latest donations
- **Impact Summary**: How your donations helped

### Filtering and Searching

- **Filter by Status**: View only completed, pending, or failed donations
- **Filter by Campaign**: See donations to specific campaigns
- **Search**: Find donations by reference number or campaign name
- **Sort**: Order by date, amount, or status

## Anonymous Donations

### How Anonymous Donations Work

When you check the "Anonymous Donation" box:

- Your name is hidden from public donor lists
- Your donation amount still counts toward campaign goals
- Campaign administrators can still see your information
- You still receive email confirmations and receipts
- Your donation appears as "Anonymous Donor" publicly

### When to Use Anonymous

Consider donating anonymously if you:

- Prefer privacy
- Don't want public recognition
- Want to support quietly
- Have personal reasons for discretion

### What Remains Visible

Even with anonymous donations:

- Campaign administrators see your full details
- Your donation history (if logged in) shows the donation
- Email confirmations are still sent to you
- Tax receipts (if applicable) include your information

## Donation Receipts

### Receiving Receipts

After your donation is verified:

1. **Email Receipt**: Sent automatically to your email
2. **Download Option**: Available in your donation history
3. **Receipt Details**: Includes donation amount, date, campaign, reference number

### Receipt Information

Official donation receipts include:

- Donor name (unless anonymous)
- Donation amount
- Campaign name and details
- Transaction date
- Reference number
- Organization information
- Receipt number

### Requesting Receipts

If you didn't receive a receipt:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Log in and view donation history
3. Click **Download Receipt** next to the donation
4. Contact administrators if still unavailable

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### "GCash payment is currently unavailable"

**Cause**: No active GCash configuration  
**Solution**: Contact administrators or try again later

#### "You do not have permission to view this donation"

**Cause**: Session expired or incorrect email  
**Solution**: 
- Use the link from your confirmation email
- Ensure you're logged in (if registered)
- Contact support with your reference number

#### "Payment proof upload failed"

**Cause**: File too large or wrong format  
**Solution**:
- Ensure file is under 5MB
- Use JPG, PNG, or GIF format
- Try compressing the image
- Ensure file is not corrupted

#### "Reference number is required"

**Cause**: Missing GCash transaction reference  
**Solution**:
- Check your GCash transaction history
- Find the reference number on your receipt
- Enter it exactly as shown

#### "Donation status stuck on Pending Verification"

**Cause**: Awaiting administrator review  
**Solution**:
- Wait 24-48 hours for verification
- Check your email for updates
- Contact administrators if delayed beyond 48 hours

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues:

1. **Check Email**: Look for automated messages with instructions
2. **Review Instructions**: Re-read the payment instructions carefully
3. **Contact Support**: Use the feedback form or contact administrators
4. **Provide Details**: Include your reference number and donation ID

## Tips for Successful Donations

1. **Double-Check Amount**: Ensure you send the exact amount
2. **Save Everything**: Keep screenshots and reference numbers
3. **Use Clear Screenshots**: Make sure all details are visible
4. **Act Promptly**: Upload proof soon after payment
5. **Check Email**: Monitor for confirmation messages
6. **Verify Campaign**: Ensure the campaign is active and accepting donations
7. **Keep Records**: Save receipts for your records

## Donation Security

### Your Information is Protected

- All payment processing is done through GCash's secure platform
- Personal information is encrypted
- Payment proofs are stored securely
- Only authorized administrators can verify donations

### Safe Donation Practices

- Only donate through official NORSU Alumni System pages
- Verify the GCash account details match official information
- Don't share your GCash PIN or password
- Keep your transaction references private
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I donate without registering?**  
A: Yes, public campaigns accept donations from non-registered users. You'll need to provide your name and email.

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Typically 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email once verified.

**Q: Can I cancel a donation?**  
A: Once payment is made, donations cannot be automatically cancelled. Contact administrators for assistance.

**Q: What if I sent the wrong amount?**  
A: Contact administrators immediately with your reference number. They can help resolve the issue.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund policies vary by campaign. Contact administrators to discuss your situation.

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**  
A: Tax deductibility depends on the organization's status and local laws. Check with administrators.

**Q: Can I donate to multiple campaigns?**  
A: Yes! Each donation is processed separately. You can support as many campaigns as you like.

**Q: What if the campaign ends before I donate?**  
A: Ended campaigns typically stop accepting donations. Choose an active campaign instead.

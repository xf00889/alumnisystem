# Donations

The Donations feature enables alumni and supporters to contribute financially to various fundraising campaigns and initiatives within the NORSU Alumni System.

## Overview

The NORSU Alumni System's donation platform provides a secure and transparent way to support causes that matter to the alumni community. Whether you're funding scholarships, supporting infrastructure projects, or contributing to emergency relief efforts, the system makes it easy to give back.

## Key Features

- **Campaign Browsing**: Discover active fundraising campaigns
- **Secure Payments**: GCash integration for safe transactions
- **Real-Time Progress**: Track campaign goals and achievements
- **Donation History**: View all your past contributions
- **Anonymous Options**: Donate privately if preferred
- **Receipt Generation**: Automatic receipts for all donations
- **Public Access**: Non-registered users can also donate

## How It Works

### The Donation Process

1. **Browse Campaigns** - Find a cause you want to support
2. **Enter Details** - Fill out the donation form with your information
3. **Make Payment** - Complete payment via GCash using the provided QR code
4. **Upload Proof** - Submit your GCash receipt screenshot
5. **Get Verified** - Administrators verify your payment
6. **Receive Confirmation** - Get email confirmation and receipt

### Payment Method

Currently, the system supports:

- **GCash**: Mobile wallet payment via QR code or account number

### Campaign Types

Common campaign categories include:

- **Scholarship Fund**: Support student education
- **Infrastructure Development**: Building and facility improvements
- **Event Funding**: Alumni gatherings and activities
- **Emergency Relief**: Disaster response and urgent needs
- **Alumni Projects**: Community-driven initiatives
- **General Fund**: Flexible funding for various needs

## Getting Started

### As a Registered Alumni

1. **Log In**: Access your account
2. **Browse Campaigns**: Navigate to Donations → Browse Campaigns
3. **Choose Campaign**: Select a cause to support
4. **Make Donation**: Follow the donation process
5. **Track History**: View your contributions in Donation History

### As a Public User

1. **Visit Website**: Go to the NORSU Alumni System
2. **Find Campaigns**: Navigate to Donations or Campaigns
3. **Select Campaign**: Choose a public campaign
4. **Donate**: Complete the donation process with your email
5. **Check Email**: Monitor for confirmation and updates

## Donation Features Explained

### Campaign Progress Tracking

Each campaign displays:

- **Goal Amount**: Target fundraising amount
- **Current Amount**: Total raised so far
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of progress
- **Percentage**: How close to the goal
- **Donor Count**: Number of contributors
- **Days Remaining**: Time left (if applicable)

### Anonymous Donations

You can choose to donate anonymously:

- Your name won't appear in public donor lists
- Your contribution still counts toward the goal
- You still receive confirmation and receipts
- Administrators can still see your information for verification

### Donation Verification

All donations go through verification:

1. **Pending Payment**: Waiting for GCash payment
2. **Pending Verification**: Payment proof submitted
3. **Completed**: Verified and processed
4. **Failed**: Could not be verified

### Receipts and Confirmations

After verification, you receive:

- Email confirmation
- Official donation receipt
- Campaign impact updates
- Thank you message

## Tips for Donors

1. **Research Campaigns**: Read full descriptions before donating
2. **Save Receipts**: Keep GCash screenshots and reference numbers
3. **Check Progress**: Monitor campaign updates
4. **Donate Promptly**: Upload payment proof soon after paying
5. **Verify Details**: Ensure GCash account details are correct
6. **Keep Records**: Save email confirmations and receipts
7. **Ask Questions**: Contact administrators if unsure

## Donation Limits and Guidelines

### Minimum and Maximum

- **Minimum**: ₱1.00 per donation
- **Maximum**: No system limit (GCash limits may apply)

### Multiple Donations

- You can donate to multiple campaigns
- You can donate multiple times to the same campaign
- Each donation is processed separately

### Campaign Restrictions

- Some campaigns are for registered alumni only
- Ended campaigns don't accept new donations
- Paused campaigns temporarily stop accepting donations

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to register to donate?**  
A: No, public campaigns accept donations from anyone. However, registered alumni get additional features like donation history tracking.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Currently, only GCash is supported for donations.

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Typically 24-48 hours after submitting payment proof.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund policies vary by campaign. Contact administrators to discuss your situation.

**Q: Are donations tax-deductible?**  
A: This depends on the organization's tax status and local laws. Check with administrators for specific information.

**Q: What if I made a mistake?**  
A: Contact administrators immediately with your reference number. They can help resolve issues.

**Q: Can I donate on behalf of someone else?**  
A: Yes, you can add a dedication message when donating.

**Q: How do I know my donation was received?**  
A: You'll receive email confirmations at each step: submission, verification, and completion.

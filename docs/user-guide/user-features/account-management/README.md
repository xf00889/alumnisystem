# Account Management

## Overview

Account Management features allow you to control access to your NORSU Alumni System account, manage your security settings, and maintain your account credentials. These features ensure your account remains secure while providing convenient access to the system.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All registered users** who have created an account
- **Users who need to access their account** or manage security settings
- **Users who have forgotten their password** and need to reset it

## Key Features

- **Login and Logout**: Securely access and exit your account
- **Password Management**: Change or reset your password
- **Email Verification**: Verify your email address for account security
- **Remember Me**: Stay logged in on trusted devices
- **Security Monitoring**: Track login attempts and account activity

## Available Account Management Features

### [Login and Logout](login-logout.md)

### [Password Management](password-management.md)

### [Email Verification](email-verification.md)

## Security Best Practices

- **Use Strong Passwords**: Create passwords with at least 8 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- **Never Share Credentials**: Keep your login information private
- **Log Out on Shared Devices**: Always log out when using public or shared computers
- **Verify Email Promptly**: Complete email verification as soon as possible after registration
- **Update Password Regularly**: Change your password periodically for better security
- **Monitor Account Activity**: Check for any suspicious login attempts

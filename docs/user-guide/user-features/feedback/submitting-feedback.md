# Submitting Feedback

## Overview

The feedback feature allows you to share your thoughts, report issues, request new features, or provide general comments about the NORSU Alumni System. Your feedback helps us improve the platform and better serve the alumni community. All feedback submissions are reviewed by system administrators who can track, prioritize, and respond to your input.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **User Role**: All authenticated users (registered alumni)
- **Permissions Required**: Active user account
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Must be logged in to the system
  - Email address must be verified

## How to Access

1. Log in to your NORSU Alumni account
2. Click on your profile icon or name in the top navigation bar
3. Select **"Feedback"** or **"Submit Feedback"** from the dropdown menu
4. You will be directed to the feedback submission form

**Alternative Access**:
- Some pages may have a "Send Feedback" link in the footer
- Navigate directly to: `/feedback/submit/`

## Key Features

- **Multiple Feedback Categories**: Choose from General, Bug Report, Feature Request, Content, or Other
- **Priority Levels**: Indicate the urgency of your feedback (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
- **File Attachments**: Upload supporting documents or screenshots (up to 5MB)
- **Feedback Tracking**: View all your submitted feedback and their current status
- **Secure Submission**: reCAPTCHA protection prevents spam and abuse
- **Engagement Points**: Earn points for contributing feedback to the community

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Submitting New Feedback

Submit feedback to share your thoughts or report issues with the system.

1. **Navigate to the Feedback Form**
   - Click on your profile menu in the top navigation
   - Select "Submit Feedback" from the dropdown
   - **Expected Result**: The feedback submission form appears
   - ![Feedback form access](../../screenshots/feedback/access-form.png)

2. **Select a Feedback Category**
   - Click on the "Category" dropdown menu
   - Choose the most appropriate category:
     - **General**: General comments or suggestions
     - **Bug Report**: Report technical issues or errors
     - **Feature Request**: Suggest new features or improvements
     - **Content**: Feedback about content quality or accuracy
     - **Other**: Feedback that doesn't fit other categories
   - **Expected Result**: The selected category is highlighted
   - ![Category selection](../../screenshots/feedback/select-category.png)

3. **Enter a Subject Line**
   - Type a brief, descriptive summary of your feedback (max 200 characters)
   - Be specific and concise
   - Example: "Unable to upload profile photo" or "Suggestion: Add dark mode"
   - **Expected Result**: Character count updates as you type
   - ![Subject entry](../../screenshots/feedback/enter-subject.png)

4. **Write Your Detailed Message**
   - In the "Message" text area, provide comprehensive details about your feedback
   - For bug reports, include:
     - What you were trying to do
     - What happened instead
     - Steps to reproduce the issue
     - Browser and device information (if relevant)
   - For feature requests, explain:
     - What feature you'd like to see
     - Why it would be beneficial
     - How you envision it working
   - **Expected Result**: Your message appears in the text area with character count
   - ![Message entry](../../screenshots/feedback/enter-message.png)

5. **Set Priority Level**
   - Select the urgency of your feedback from the "Priority" dropdown:
     - **Low**: Minor suggestions or non-urgent feedback
     - **Medium**: Standard feedback or moderate issues
     - **High**: Important issues affecting functionality
     - **Urgent**: Critical bugs or security concerns
   - **Expected Result**: Priority level is selected
   - ![Priority selection](../../screenshots/feedback/select-priority.png)

6. **Attach Supporting Files (Optional)**
   - Click the "Choose File" button under "Attachment"
   - Select a file from your device
   - Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, PNG, JPG, JPEG
   - Maximum file size: 5MB
   - **Best Practice**: Attach screenshots for bug reports or visual suggestions
   - **Expected Result**: File name appears next to the button
   - ![File attachment](../../screenshots/feedback/attach-file.png)

7. **Complete Security Verification**
   - If reCAPTCHA is enabled, the verification will process automatically
   - Wait for the green checkmark to appear
   - **Expected Result**: reCAPTCHA verification completes successfully
   - ![reCAPTCHA verification](../../screenshots/feedback/recaptcha.png)

8. **Submit Your Feedback**
   - Review all information for accuracy
   - Click the **"Submit Feedback"** button
   - **Expected Result**: Success message appears confirming submission
   - You are redirected to "My Feedbacks" page
   - You earn 5 engagement points for submitting feedback
   - ![Submission success](../../screenshots/feedback/submit-success.png)

### Task 2: Viewing Your Submitted Feedback

Track the status of all feedback you've submitted to the system.

1. **Access Your Feedback List**
   - Click on your profile menu in the top navigation
   - Select "My Feedbacks" from the dropdown
   - Alternatively, after submitting feedback, you're automatically redirected here
   - **Expected Result**: A list of all your submitted feedback appears
   - ![My feedbacks list](../../screenshots/feedback/my-feedbacks.png)

2. **Review Feedback Details**
   - Each feedback entry displays:
     - **Category badge**: Color-coded by category type
     - **Subject**: Your feedback title
     - **Status**: Current processing status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved, Closed)
     - **Priority**: Urgency level you assigned
     - **Date submitted**: When you created the feedback
   - **Expected Result**: All your feedback is visible with current status
   - ![Feedback details](../../screenshots/feedback/feedback-details.png)

3. **View Individual Feedback**
   - Click on any feedback entry to view full details
   - See your complete message and any attached files
   - View admin notes if administrators have added responses
   - **Expected Result**: Full feedback details are displayed
   - ![View feedback detail](../../screenshots/feedback/view-detail.png)

4. **Navigate Through Pages**
   - If you have more than 10 feedback submissions, use pagination controls
   - Click page numbers or "Next"/"Previous" buttons
   - **Expected Result**: Additional feedback entries load
   - ![Pagination](../../screenshots/feedback/pagination.png)

### Task 3: Understanding Feedback Status

Learn what each status means for your submitted feedback.

1. **Pending**
   - Your feedback has been submitted and is awaiting review
   - Administrators have not yet reviewed it
   - **Action**: No action needed; wait for admin review

2. **In Progress**
   - Administrators are actively working on your feedback
   - For bug reports: The issue is being investigated or fixed
   - For feature requests: The suggestion is being evaluated or developed
   - **Action**: Monitor for updates; administrators may add notes

3. **Resolved**
   - Your feedback has been addressed
   - For bug reports: The issue has been fixed
   - For feature requests: The feature has been implemented or a decision has been made
   - Check admin notes for details about the resolution
   - **Action**: Verify the resolution if applicable

4. **Closed**
   - The feedback has been reviewed and closed
   - May indicate: duplicate feedback, not actionable, or completed
   - Check admin notes for explanation
   - **Action**: No further action needed

## Tips and Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: Provide clear, detailed information to help administrators understand your feedback
- **One Topic Per Submission**: Submit separate feedback for different issues or suggestions
- **Include Screenshots**: For bug reports or UI suggestions, attach screenshots showing the issue
- **Check Existing Feedback**: Before submitting, consider if your feedback might already be submitted
- **Use Appropriate Categories**: Select the correct category to help prioritize your feedback
- **Set Realistic Priorities**: Reserve "Urgent" for critical issues; most feedback should be "Medium"
- **Follow Up**: Check "My Feedbacks" periodically to see if administrators have added notes
- **Provide Context**: Include information about your browser, device, or specific circumstances
- **Be Constructive**: Frame feedback positively and suggest solutions when possible

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Reporting a Bug
You encounter an error when trying to upload a document to your profile.

**Steps**:
1. Select "Bug Report" as the category
2. Subject: "Error uploading PDF document to profile"
3. Message: Describe what happened, what you expected, and steps to reproduce
4. Priority: "High" (if it prevents important functionality)
5. Attach a screenshot of the error message
6. Submit the feedback

### Use Case 2: Requesting a New Feature
You'd like to see a mobile app for the alumni system.

**Steps**:
1. Select "Feature Request" as the category
2. Subject: "Mobile app for iOS and Android"
3. Message: Explain why a mobile app would be beneficial and what features it should include
4. Priority: "Low" or "Medium"
5. Submit the feedback

### Use Case 3: Providing General Feedback
You want to compliment the recent website redesign.

**Steps**:
1. Select "General" as the category
2. Subject: "Positive feedback on new design"
3. Message: Share what you like about the changes
4. Priority: "Low"
5. Submit the feedback

### Use Case 4: Reporting Content Issues
You notice outdated information on the About page.

**Steps**:
1. Select "Content" as the category
2. Subject: "Outdated information on About page"
3. Message: Specify what information is incorrect and what it should be
4. Priority: "Medium"
5. Attach a screenshot highlighting the issue
6. Submit the feedback

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot Submit Feedback - Form Validation Errors
**Symptoms**: Error messages appear when trying to submit the form
**Solution**: 
- Ensure all required fields are filled (Category, Subject, Message, Priority)
- Check that your subject is under 200 characters
- Verify your message is not empty
- If attaching a file, ensure it's under 5MB and in an accepted format

### Issue: File Upload Fails
**Symptoms**: Error message about file size or type when attaching a file
**Solution**:
- Check file size is under 5MB (compress large images if needed)
- Verify file format is one of: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, PNG, JPG, JPEG
- Try a different file or convert to an accepted format
- Ensure the file is not corrupted

### Issue: reCAPTCHA Verification Fails
**Symptoms**: Cannot submit form due to reCAPTCHA error
**Solution**:
- Refresh the page and try again
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser if the issue persists
- Contact administrators if reCAPTCHA consistently fails

### Issue: Cannot Find Submitted Feedback
**Symptoms**: Feedback doesn't appear in "My Feedbacks" after submission
**Solution**:
- Refresh the "My Feedbacks" page
- Check if you received a success message after submission
- Verify you're logged in with the same account used to submit
- If feedback is still missing, submit a new feedback reporting the issue

### Issue: No Response to Urgent Feedback
**Symptoms**: Urgent feedback remains in "Pending" status for extended period
**Solution**:
- Allow 24-48 hours for initial review, even for urgent items
- Check if admin notes have been added with updates
- For truly critical issues (security, data loss), contact administrators directly
- Consider submitting additional feedback if no response after reasonable time

## Additional Notes

- **Privacy**: Your feedback is visible to system administrators but not to other users
- **Engagement Points**: You earn 5 points for each feedback submission, contributing to your engagement score
- **Response Time**: Administrators review feedback regularly, but response times vary based on priority and workload
- **Anonymous Feedback**: Currently, all feedback requires authentication; anonymous submissions are not supported
- **Feedback History**: All your submitted feedback is retained and accessible in "My Feedbacks"
- **Admin Notes**: Administrators may add notes to your feedback; check back periodically for updates
- **Duplicate Feedback**: If your feedback is similar to existing submissions, it may be marked as duplicate and closed

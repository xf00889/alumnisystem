# Work Experience

## Overview

The Work Experience feature allows you to document and showcase your professional career history. You can add multiple positions, track career progression, mark your current role, and highlight significant career milestones. This information helps other alumni understand your professional journey and can assist with networking and mentorship opportunities.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All authenticated users** with verified email addresses
- **Alumni who want to showcase their career history**
- **Users seeking networking and mentorship opportunities**

## Prerequisites

- Active account with verified email
- Completed registration process
- Logged into the system

## How to Access

1. Log into your NORSU Alumni System account
2. Navigate to your profile page
3. Click the "Edit Profile" button
4. Scroll to the "Work Experience" section
5. Or visit: `/accounts/profile/update/` and navigate to the Experience tab

## Key Features

- **Multiple Experience Entries**: Add unlimited work positions
- **Current Position Tracking**: Mark your current role
- **Career Significance**: Highlight promotions and career milestones
- **Date Ranges**: Track start and end dates for each position
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Add job responsibilities and achievements
- **Skills Gained**: Document skills acquired in each role
- **Salary Range**: Optionally track compensation (private)
- **Location Tracking**: Record where you worked
- **Chronological Display**: Experiences sorted by date
- **Edit and Delete**: Modify or remove experience entries

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Adding a New Work Experience Entry

1. **Navigate to the Work Experience section**
   - Go to your profile edit page
   - Find the "Work Experience" or "Experience" section
   - **Expected Result**: Experience section is displayed with existing entries

2. **Click "Add Experience" button**
   - Look for the "Add Experience" or "+" button
   - **Expected Result**: Experience form appears

3. **Enter the company name**
   - Type the name of the company or organization
   - Use the official company name
   - **Expected Result**: Company name is entered

4. **Enter your position or job title**
   - Type your job title (e.g., "Software Engineer", "Marketing Manager")
   - Use the official title from your employment
   - **Expected Result**: Position is entered


5. **Enter the location**
   - Type the city and country where you worked
   - Example: "Manila, Philippines" or "Remote"
   - **Expected Result**: Location is entered

6. **Select the start date**
   - Click the start date field
   - Choose the month and year you started
   - **Expected Result**: Start date is selected

7. **Select the end date or mark as current**
   - If this is your current position, check "I currently work here"
   - If it's a past position, select the end date
   - **Expected Result**: End date is set or current position is marked

8. **Add a description (optional)**
   - Describe your responsibilities and duties
   - Use bullet points for clarity
   - Include key projects and accomplishments
   - **Expected Result**: Description is entered

9. **Add achievements (optional)**
   - Document specific accomplishments
   - Include metrics and results when possible
   - Examples: "Increased sales by 30%", "Led team of 5 developers"
   - **Expected Result**: Achievements are entered

10. **Select career significance**
    - Choose the type of career move:
      - **Regular Position**: Standard role
      - **Promotion**: Advancement within company
      - **Lateral Move**: Same level, different role
      - **New Role**: New type of position
      - **Company Change**: Move to new organization
    - **Expected Result**: Career significance is selected

11. **Add skills gained (optional)**
    - List skills you developed in this role
    - Separate skills with commas
    - Example: "Project Management, Team Leadership, Python"
    - **Expected Result**: Skills are entered

12. **Select salary range (optional)**
    - Choose your compensation range
    - This information is kept private
    - Select "Prefer not to say" if you don't want to share
    - **Expected Result**: Salary range is selected

13. **Save the experience entry**
    - Click "Save" or "Add Experience" button
    - **Expected Result**: Success message appears, experience is added

### Task 2: Editing an Existing Experience Entry

1. **Locate the experience entry to edit**
   - Scroll to the Work Experience section
   - Find the entry you want to modify
   - **Expected Result**: Experience entry is visible

2. **Click the "Edit" button**
   - Look for the edit icon (pencil) or "Edit" button
   - **Expected Result**: Experience form opens with current information

3. **Modify the information**
   - Update any fields: company, position, dates, description, etc.
   - **Expected Result**: Fields are updated with new information

4. **Save your changes**
   - Click "Save" or "Update" button
   - **Expected Result**: Success message appears, changes are saved


### Task 3: Marking Your Current Position

1. **Add or edit an experience entry**
   - Create a new entry or edit an existing one
   - **Expected Result**: Experience form is open

2. **Check "I currently work here"**
   - Find the "Current Position" or "I currently work here" checkbox
   - Check the box
   - **Expected Result**: Checkbox is selected, end date field is disabled

3. **Verify end date is cleared**
   - End date should be automatically cleared
   - You cannot set an end date for current positions
   - **Expected Result**: End date is empty

4. **Save the entry**
   - Click "Save" button
   - Any previous current position is automatically unmarked
   - Your profile's "Current Position" field is updated
   - **Expected Result**: Current position is set

**Note**: You can only have one current position at a time. Marking a new position as current will automatically unmark the previous one.

### Task 4: Setting Career Significance

Career significance helps highlight important career milestones and progression.

1. **Edit an experience entry**
   - Open the experience you want to mark
   - **Expected Result**: Experience form is displayed

2. **Select the appropriate career significance**
   - **Regular Position**: Standard employment, no special significance
   - **Promotion**: You were promoted within the same company
   - **Lateral Move**: Moved to a different role at the same level
   - **New Role**: Started a completely new type of position
   - **Company Change**: Changed employers
   - **Expected Result**: Career significance is selected

3. **Save the entry**
   - Click "Save" button
   - Significant career moves may be highlighted on your profile
   - **Expected Result**: Career significance is saved

### Task 5: Deleting an Experience Entry

1. **Locate the experience entry to delete**
   - Scroll to the Work Experience section
   - Find the entry you want to remove
   - **Expected Result**: Experience entry is visible

2. **Click the "Delete" button**
   - Look for the delete icon (trash can) or "Delete" button
   - **Expected Result**: Confirmation dialog appears

3. **Confirm deletion**
   - Read the confirmation message
   - Click "Yes" or "Confirm" to proceed
   - Click "No" or "Cancel" to abort
   - **Expected Result**: Entry is deleted if confirmed

4. **Verify deletion**
   - Check that the entry no longer appears
   - **Expected Result**: Experience entry is removed from profile

**Important**: If you delete your current position, your profile's "Current Position" field will be cleared.


## Tips and Best Practices

- **Be Comprehensive**: Include all relevant work experience, not just current role
- **Use Action Verbs**: Start descriptions with strong action verbs (Led, Managed, Developed)
- **Quantify Achievements**: Include numbers and metrics when possible
- **Keep Current**: Update when you change jobs or get promoted
- **Mark Current Position**: Always mark your current role
- **Highlight Progression**: Use career significance to show growth
- **Add Skills**: Document skills gained in each role
- **Be Honest**: Only include actual work experience
- **Professional Tone**: Use professional language in descriptions
- **Update Regularly**: Review and update quarterly

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot mark as current position
**Symptoms**: Current position checkbox is disabled or not working

**Solution**:
- Ensure you're editing an experience entry
- Check that end date is not set
- Try unchecking and rechecking the box
- Refresh the page and try again
- Only one position can be current at a time
- Contact support if issue persists

### Issue: End date required error
**Symptoms**: System requires end date even for current position

**Solution**:
- Ensure "I currently work here" is checked
- End date should be automatically cleared
- Try unchecking and rechecking current position
- Refresh the page
- Contact support if validation error persists

### Issue: Dates in wrong order
**Symptoms**: "End date must be after start date" error

**Solution**:
- Verify start date is before end date
- Check that dates are in correct format
- For current positions, check "I currently work here"
- Clear end date if it's your current role
- Double-check month and year selections

### Issue: Cannot save experience entry
**Symptoms**: Save button doesn't work or entry doesn't save

**Solution**:
- Check for validation errors (red text)
- Ensure required fields are filled (company, position, location, start date)
- Verify date format is correct
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing and re-entering information
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if problem continues

### Issue: Current position not updating profile
**Symptoms**: Profile still shows old current position

**Solution**:
- Ensure new position is marked as current
- Refresh your profile page
- Wait a few seconds for changes to propagate
- Check that only one position is marked as current
- Contact support if profile doesn't update


### Issue: Skills not saving
**Symptoms**: Skills gained field doesn't save

**Solution**:
- Ensure skills are comma-separated
- Don't use special characters
- Keep skill names concise
- Try saving without skills first, then edit to add
- Contact support if issue persists

## Field Descriptions

### Required Fields
- **Company**: Name of the organization (required)
- **Position**: Your job title (required)
- **Location**: City and country where you worked (required)
- **Start Date**: When you started the position (required)

### Optional Fields
- **End Date**: When you left the position (required for past positions, auto-cleared for current)
- **Description**: Your responsibilities and duties
- **Achievements**: Specific accomplishments and results
- **Career Significance**: Type of career move
- **Skills Gained**: Skills developed in this role
- **Salary Range**: Compensation range (private)

### Checkboxes
- **I currently work here**: Marks this as your current position

## Career Significance Options

### Regular Position
- Standard employment with no special significance
- Most common selection
- Use for typical job changes

### Promotion
- Advancement within the same company
- Higher level or increased responsibility
- Highlights career growth

### Lateral Move
- Different role at similar level
- Career pivot or skill diversification
- Shows adaptability

### New Role
- Completely new type of position
- Career change or new industry
- Demonstrates versatility

### Company Change
- Move to a different organization
- New employer at similar or different level
- Shows market value

## Salary Range Options

- Below ₱15,000
- ₱15,000 - ₱30,000
- ₱30,000 - ₱50,000
- ₱50,000 - ₱80,000
- ₱80,000 - ₱100,000
- Above ₱100,000
- Prefer not to say

**Note**: Salary information is kept strictly private and used only for statistical purposes. It is never displayed publicly.

## Additional Notes

### Experience Display
- Experiences are sorted chronologically (most recent first)
- Current position is highlighted with a badge
- Career significance may be indicated with icons
- All experiences appear on your public profile (if profile is public)

### Data Privacy
- Salary range is never displayed publicly
- Only visible to you and system administrators
- Used for aggregate statistics only
- You can always select "Prefer not to say"

### Profile Integration
- Current position updates your profile's "Current Position" field
- Current employer updates your profile's "Current Employer" field
- Changes sync with Alumni directory information
- Skills gained can be added to your skills list

### Best Practices for Descriptions
- Use bullet points for readability
- Start with action verbs (Managed, Developed, Led, Implemented)
- Include specific responsibilities
- Mention team size if you managed people
- Highlight key projects and initiatives
- Keep it concise but informative

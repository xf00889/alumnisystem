# Personal Information

## Overview

The Personal Information feature allows you to update and maintain your basic profile information, contact details, and location data. Keeping this information current helps other alumni find and connect with you, and ensures you receive important communications from the system.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All authenticated users** with verified email addresses
- **Alumni who have completed registration**
- **Users who want to update their profile information**

## Prerequisites

- Active account with verified email
- Completed registration process
- Logged into the system

## How to Access

1. Log into your NORSU Alumni System account
2. Click on your profile icon or name in the top navigation bar
3. Select "Profile" or "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
4. Click the "Edit Profile" button or navigate to the "Personal Information" tab
5. Or visit: `/accounts/profile/update/`

## Key Features

- **Basic Information**: Update name, birth date, gender, and bio
- **Contact Information**: Manage phone number and email
- **Location Information**: Update address, city, state, country, and postal code
- **Social Media Profiles**: Add LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter links
- **Professional Information**: Update current position and employer
- **Privacy Settings**: Control profile visibility
- **Real-time Validation**: Instant feedback on form inputs
- **Auto-save**: Changes are saved automatically

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Updating Basic Information

1. **Navigate to the profile edit page**
   - Click "Edit Profile" from your profile page
   - Or go to the Personal Information section
   - **Expected Result**: Edit form is displayed with current information

2. **Update your first and last name**
   - Enter your first name in the "First Name" field
   - Enter your last name in the "Last Name" field
   - Names should match your official records
   - **Expected Result**: Name fields are updated

3. **Set or update your birth date**
   - Click on the birth date field
   - Select your date of birth from the calendar picker
   - Or manually enter in YYYY-MM-DD format
   - **Expected Result**: Birth date is selected

4. **Select your gender**
   - Choose from: Male, Female, Other, or Prefer not to say
   - This field is optional
   - **Expected Result**: Gender is selected


5. **Write or update your bio**
   - Enter a brief description about yourself (up to 500 characters)
   - Share your interests, achievements, or professional summary
   - This appears on your public profile
   - **Expected Result**: Bio is updated

6. **Save your changes**
   - Click the "Save" or "Update Profile" button
   - **Expected Result**: Success message appears, changes are saved

### Task 2: Updating Contact Information

1. **Update your phone number**
   - Enter your phone number in international format (e.g., +1-555-123-4567)
   - Include country code for international numbers
   - This field is optional
   - **Expected Result**: Phone number is formatted and saved

2. **Verify your email address**
   - Your primary email is displayed (cannot be changed here)
   - To change email, use Account Settings
   - **Expected Result**: Current email is shown

3. **Add social media profiles**
   - Enter your LinkedIn profile URL (e.g., https://linkedin.com/in/yourprofile)
   - Enter your Facebook profile URL (e.g., https://facebook.com/yourprofile)
   - Enter your Twitter profile URL (e.g., https://twitter.com/yourprofile)
   - URLs are automatically validated
   - **Expected Result**: Social media links are saved

4. **Save contact information**
   - Click "Save" to update your contact details
   - **Expected Result**: Contact information is updated successfully

### Task 3: Updating Location Information

1. **Enter your street address**
   - Type your full street address
   - Include apartment/unit number if applicable
   - This field is optional
   - **Expected Result**: Address is entered

2. **Enter your city**
   - Type the name of your city
   - Use proper capitalization
   - **Expected Result**: City is entered

3. **Enter your state or province**
   - Type your state, province, or region
   - **Expected Result**: State/province is entered

4. **Select your country**
   - Click the country dropdown
   - Select your country from the list
   - Countries are listed alphabetically
   - **Expected Result**: Country is selected

5. **Enter your postal code**
   - Type your ZIP code or postal code
   - Format varies by country
   - **Expected Result**: Postal code is entered

6. **Save location information**
   - Click "Save" to update your location
   - Your location appears on your profile
   - **Expected Result**: Location information is saved


### Task 4: Updating Professional Information

1. **Enter your current position**
   - Type your current job title (e.g., Software Engineer)
   - This appears on your profile
   - **Expected Result**: Current position is entered

2. **Enter your current employer**
   - Type the name of your current company or organization
   - **Expected Result**: Current employer is entered

3. **Enter your industry**
   - Type the industry you work in (e.g., Information Technology)
   - **Expected Result**: Industry is entered

4. **Select your employment status**
   - Choose from:
     - Employed Full-Time
     - Employed Part-Time
     - Self-Employed
     - Unemployed
     - Further Studies
     - Retired
     - Internship/OJT
   - **Expected Result**: Employment status is selected

5. **Select your salary range (optional)**
   - Choose from predefined ranges
   - This information is kept private
   - Used for statistical purposes only
   - Select "Prefer not to say" if you don't want to share
   - **Expected Result**: Salary range is selected

6. **Save professional information**
   - Click "Save" to update your professional details
   - **Expected Result**: Professional information is saved

### Task 5: Managing Privacy Settings

1. **Set profile visibility**
   - Check "Make profile public" to allow other alumni to view your profile
   - Uncheck to make your profile private (visible only to you)
   - **Expected Result**: Privacy setting is updated

2. **Understand what's visible**
   - Public profiles: Visible to all authenticated alumni
   - Private profiles: Only visible to you and administrators
   - Contact information visibility can be controlled separately
   - **Expected Result**: You understand privacy implications

3. **Save privacy settings**
   - Click "Save" to apply privacy changes
   - **Expected Result**: Privacy settings are saved

## Tips and Best Practices

- **Keep Information Current**: Update your profile whenever your contact or employment information changes
- **Use Professional Photo**: Upload a clear, professional profile photo
- **Complete All Fields**: A complete profile helps other alumni find and connect with you
- **Verify URLs**: Ensure social media links are correct and active
- **Use International Format**: For phone numbers, include country code
- **Be Professional**: Remember your profile is visible to other alumni and potential employers
- **Update Regularly**: Review and update your information at least quarterly
- **Privacy Awareness**: Consider what information you want to share publicly


## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: New Job or Career Change
When you start a new job, update your current position, employer, industry, and employment status to reflect your new role.

### Use Case 2: Relocation
If you move to a new city or country, update your address, city, state, country, and postal code to keep your location current.

### Use Case 3: Contact Information Change
When you get a new phone number or want to add social media profiles, update your contact information section.

### Use Case 4: Privacy Adjustment
If you want to control who can see your profile, adjust your privacy settings to make your profile public or private.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Changes not saving
**Symptoms**: Click save but information doesn't update

**Solution**:
- Check for validation errors (red text near fields)
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and re-entering information
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Contact support if issue persists

### Issue: Phone number format error
**Symptoms**: "Invalid phone number" error message

**Solution**:
- Use international format: +[country code]-[area code]-[number]
- Example: +1-555-123-4567
- Remove spaces and special characters except + and -
- Ensure country code is included
- Try entering just numbers if format issues persist

### Issue: Social media URL not accepted
**Symptoms**: "Invalid URL" error for social media links

**Solution**:
- Ensure URL starts with http:// or https://
- Copy the full URL from your browser address bar
- Check for typos in the URL
- Ensure the profile exists and is public
- Example format: https://linkedin.com/in/yourprofile

### Issue: Country not in dropdown
**Symptoms**: Cannot find your country in the list

**Solution**:
- Scroll through the entire list (alphabetically ordered)
- Try searching by typing the first letter
- Check spelling of country name
- Contact support if country is genuinely missing

### Issue: Cannot change email address
**Symptoms**: Email field is not editable

**Solution**:
- Email address cannot be changed from this page
- Go to Account Settings to change email
- See [Account Management](../account-management/README.md) for details
- Contact support for email change assistance

### Issue: Profile not appearing in directory
**Symptoms**: Other alumni cannot find your profile

**Solution**:
- Check that "Make profile public" is enabled
- Ensure you've completed all required profile sections
- Verify your account is fully activated
- Wait a few minutes for changes to propagate
- Contact support if profile remains hidden


## Field Descriptions

### Basic Information Fields
- **First Name**: Your given name (required)
- **Last Name**: Your family name (required)
- **Birth Date**: Your date of birth (optional, used for age verification)
- **Gender**: Your gender identity (optional)
- **Bio**: Brief description about yourself (max 500 characters)

### Contact Information Fields
- **Phone Number**: Your contact phone number (optional)
- **LinkedIn Profile**: Your LinkedIn profile URL (optional)
- **Facebook Profile**: Your Facebook profile URL (optional)
- **Twitter Profile**: Your Twitter profile URL (optional)

### Location Information Fields
- **Address**: Your street address (optional)
- **City**: Your city of residence (optional)
- **State/Province**: Your state or province (optional)
- **Country**: Your country of residence (optional)
- **Postal Code**: Your ZIP or postal code (optional)

### Professional Information Fields
- **Current Position**: Your current job title (optional)
- **Current Employer**: Your current company or organization (optional)
- **Industry**: The industry you work in (optional)
- **Employment Status**: Your current employment situation (optional)
- **Salary Range**: Your salary range (optional, private)

### Privacy Settings
- **Make Profile Public**: Controls whether other alumni can view your profile

## Data Privacy and Security

### What Information is Public
- Name (if profile is public)
- Bio (if profile is public)
- Current position and employer (if profile is public)
- Location (city, state, country - if profile is public)
- Social media links (if profile is public)

### What Information is Private
- Birth date (never shown publicly)
- Full street address (never shown publicly)
- Phone number (only visible to connections)
- Salary range (never shown publicly, used for statistics only)
- Email address (controlled separately in privacy settings)

### Data Protection
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access is controlled by authentication and authorization
- You can delete your account and data at any time
- Data is not shared with third parties without consent

## Additional Notes

### Profile Completeness
A complete profile includes:
- Basic information (name, bio)
- Contact information (at least one method)
- Location information (at least city and country)
- Professional information (current position and employer)
- Profile photo
- Education history
- Work experience

### Profile Visibility
- Public profiles appear in alumni directory searches
- Private profiles are hidden from directory
- Administrators can always view profiles
- Profile completeness affects search ranking

### Updating Frequency
- Review your profile quarterly
- Update immediately after major life changes (new job, relocation)
- Keep contact information current for important communications

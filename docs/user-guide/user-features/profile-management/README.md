# Profile Management

## Overview

Profile Management features allow you to maintain and showcase your personal, educational, and professional information. A complete and up-to-date profile helps you connect with other alumni, find job opportunities, and participate fully in the NORSU Alumni System.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All authenticated users** with verified email addresses
- **Alumni who have completed registration**
- **Users who want to maintain their professional presence**

## Key Features

- **Personal Information**: Update basic details, contact info, and location
- **Education History**: Document your academic background
- **Work Experience**: Track your career progression
- **Skills**: Showcase your professional and technical abilities
- **Documents**: Upload and manage important credentials
- **Profile Photo**: Add a professional profile picture
- **Privacy Controls**: Manage what information is visible to others

## Available Features

### [Personal Information](personal-information.md)
Update your basic information, contact details, location, and professional information. Control your profile visibility and privacy settings.

**Key Actions**:
- Update name, birth date, and gender
- Manage contact information and social media links
- Update location and address
- Set current position and employer
- Control profile privacy

### [Education History](education-history.md)
Manage your educational background including degrees, certifications, and academic achievements from NORSU and other institutions.

**Key Actions**:
- Add multiple education entries
- Set primary education
- Select NORSU campus and program
- Document academic achievements
- Edit and delete education records

### [Work Experience](work-experience.md)
Document your professional career history, track career progression, and highlight significant career milestones.

**Key Actions**:
- Add work experience entries
- Mark current position
- Set career significance (promotions, career changes)
- Document achievements and skills gained
- Track salary ranges (private)

### [Skills Management](skills.md)
Add and manage your professional and technical skills with proficiency levels and experience tracking.

**Key Actions**:
- Add unlimited skills
- Categorize by skill type
- Set proficiency levels (Beginner to Expert)
- Track years of experience
- Mark primary skills

### [Document Uploads](documents.md)
Upload and manage important academic and professional documents for verification and job applications.

**Key Actions**:
- Upload transcripts, certificates, diplomas, and resumes
- View and download uploaded documents
- Track verification status
- Delete documents
- Use documents for job applications

### [Profile Photo](profile-photo.md)
Upload and manage your profile picture to personalize your profile and improve visibility.

**Key Actions**:
- Upload profile photo
- Change existing photo
- Remove photo
- Preview before saving

## Getting Started

1. **Complete Basic Information**: Start by filling out your personal information
2. **Add Education**: Document your NORSU degree and other education
3. **Add Work Experience**: Include your current position and career history
4. **Upload Profile Photo**: Add a professional photo
5. **Add Skills**: List your professional and technical skills
6. **Upload Documents**: Add your resume and important credentials

## Tips for a Complete Profile

- **Fill All Sections**: Complete profiles get more visibility
- **Keep Information Current**: Update regularly when things change
- **Use Professional Photo**: Clear, professional photos work best
- **Be Comprehensive**: Include all relevant experience and skills
- **Update Documents**: Keep your resume and credentials current
- **Check Privacy Settings**: Ensure your privacy preferences are set correctly

## Profile Completeness

A complete profile includes:
- ✓ Basic information (name, bio)
- ✓ Profile photo
- ✓ Contact information
- ✓ Location information
- ✓ At least one education entry (primary education)
- ✓ Current work experience
- ✓ At least 5 skills
- ✓ Uploaded resume

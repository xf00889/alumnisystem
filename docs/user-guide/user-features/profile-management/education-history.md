# Education History

## Overview

The Education History feature allows you to add, edit, and manage your educational background. You can record multiple degrees, certifications, and educational achievements from NORSU and other institutions. Your primary education (typically your highest degree from NORSU) is highlighted on your profile.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All authenticated users** with verified email addresses
- **Alumni who have completed registration**
- **Users who want to showcase their educational background**

## Prerequisites

- Active account with verified email
- Completed registration process
- Logged into the system

## How to Access

1. Log into your NORSU Alumni System account
2. Navigate to your profile page
3. Click the "Edit Profile" button
4. Scroll to the "Education" section
5. Or visit: `/accounts/profile/update/` and navigate to the Education tab

## Key Features

- **Multiple Education Entries**: Add multiple degrees and certifications
- **Primary Education**: Designate your main NORSU degree
- **NORSU Campus Selection**: Choose from all NORSU campuses
- **Program Selection**: Select from predefined programs or add custom ones
- **Graduation Year**: Record when you graduated
- **Major/Specialization**: Add your major or area of focus
- **Achievements**: Document academic honors and achievements
- **Edit and Delete**: Modify or remove education entries
- **Chronological Display**: Education displayed by graduation year

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Adding a New Education Entry

1. **Navigate to the Education section**
   - Go to your profile edit page
   - Find the "Education" section
   - **Expected Result**: Education section is displayed with existing entries

2. **Click "Add Education" button**
   - Look for the "Add Education" or "+" button
   - **Expected Result**: Education form appears

3. **Select your program**
   - Click the "Program" dropdown
   - Choose from available programs:
     - BS in Information Technology
     - BS in Computer Science
     - BS in Accountancy
     - BS in Business Administration
     - Bachelor of Elementary Education
     - BS in Nursing
     - BS in Criminology
     - And many more...
   - Select "Other Program" if yours isn't listed
   - **Expected Result**: Program is selected

4. **Enter your major or specialization (optional)**
   - Type your major, concentration, or specialization
   - Example: "Major in Financial Management"
   - **Expected Result**: Major is entered


5. **Select your campus**
   - Click the "Campus" dropdown
   - Choose from NORSU campuses:
     - Main Campus I & II (Dumaguete)
     - Bais Campus I & II
     - Guihulngan Campus
     - Mabinay Campus
     - Bayawan-Sta. Catalina Campus
     - Siaton Campus
     - Pamplona Campus
     - Other Campus
   - **Expected Result**: Campus is selected

6. **Enter your graduation year**
   - Type the year you graduated (e.g., 2020)
   - Must be between 1970 and current year
   - **Expected Result**: Graduation year is entered

7. **Add achievements (optional)**
   - Enter any academic honors, awards, or achievements
   - Examples: "Cum Laude", "Dean's List", "Best Thesis Award"
   - **Expected Result**: Achievements are entered

8. **Set as primary education (optional)**
   - Check "Set as primary education" if this is your main NORSU degree
   - Only one education entry can be primary
   - Primary education is highlighted on your profile
   - **Expected Result**: Primary education checkbox is selected

9. **Save the education entry**
   - Click "Save" or "Add Education" button
   - **Expected Result**: Success message appears, education is added to your profile

### Task 2: Editing an Existing Education Entry

1. **Locate the education entry to edit**
   - Scroll to the Education section on your profile
   - Find the entry you want to modify
   - **Expected Result**: Education entry is visible

2. **Click the "Edit" button**
   - Look for the edit icon (pencil) or "Edit" button next to the entry
   - **Expected Result**: Education form opens with current information

3. **Modify the information**
   - Update any fields: program, major, campus, graduation year, achievements
   - Change primary education status if needed
   - **Expected Result**: Fields are updated with new information

4. **Save your changes**
   - Click "Save" or "Update" button
   - **Expected Result**: Success message appears, changes are saved

### Task 3: Setting Primary Education

Your primary education is the degree that appears most prominently on your profile and in directory listings.

1. **Identify your primary degree**
   - Usually your highest degree from NORSU
   - Or the degree most relevant to your career
   - **Expected Result**: You know which degree to set as primary

2. **Edit the education entry**
   - Click "Edit" on the education entry you want to set as primary
   - **Expected Result**: Education form opens

3. **Check "Set as primary education"**
   - Find the "Primary Education" checkbox
   - Check the box
   - **Expected Result**: Checkbox is selected

4. **Save the changes**
   - Click "Save" button
   - Any previous primary education is automatically unmarked
   - **Expected Result**: New primary education is set, old one is unmarked


### Task 4: Deleting an Education Entry

1. **Locate the education entry to delete**
   - Scroll to the Education section
   - Find the entry you want to remove
   - **Expected Result**: Education entry is visible

2. **Click the "Delete" button**
   - Look for the delete icon (trash can) or "Delete" button
   - **Expected Result**: Confirmation dialog appears

3. **Confirm deletion**
   - Read the confirmation message
   - Click "Yes" or "Confirm" to proceed
   - Click "No" or "Cancel" to abort
   - **Expected Result**: Entry is deleted if confirmed

4. **Verify deletion**
   - Check that the entry no longer appears in your education list
   - **Expected Result**: Education entry is removed from profile

**Important**: If you delete your primary education, you'll need to set another entry as primary.

## Tips and Best Practices

- **Set Primary Education**: Always designate your main NORSU degree as primary
- **Be Accurate**: Enter information exactly as it appears on your diploma
- **Include All Degrees**: Add all relevant degrees, not just your NORSU education
- **Add Achievements**: Include honors, awards, and special recognitions
- **Keep Updated**: Add new certifications and continuing education
- **Chronological Order**: System automatically sorts by graduation year
- **Complete Information**: Fill all fields for better profile visibility
- **Verify Before Saving**: Double-check information before saving

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Recent Graduate
Add your newly completed degree, set it as primary, and include any graduation honors.

### Use Case 2: Multiple Degrees
Add all your degrees (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD) and set your highest degree as primary.

### Use Case 3: Continuing Education
Add certifications, professional courses, and continuing education programs to showcase ongoing learning.

Edit existing entries to fix typos, update graduation years, or add forgotten achievements.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot find my program in the list
**Symptoms**: Your specific program is not in the dropdown

**Solution**:
- Look for a similar program name
- Select "Other Program" and specify in the major field
- Contact administrators to add your program to the list
- Use the closest matching program temporarily

### Issue: Cannot set as primary education
**Symptoms**: Primary education checkbox is disabled or not working

**Solution**:
- Ensure you're editing an existing entry (not adding new)
- Check that you have at least one education entry
- Try refreshing the page
- Only one entry can be primary at a time
- Contact support if issue persists

### Issue: Graduation year validation error
**Symptoms**: "Invalid year" error message

**Solution**:
- Enter a 4-digit year (e.g., 2020, not 20)
- Year must be between 1970 and current year
- Cannot enter future years
- Check for typos
- Use numeric characters only


### Issue: Changes not saving
**Symptoms**: Click save but education entry doesn't update

**Solution**:
- Check for validation errors (red text)
- Ensure required fields are filled (program, campus, graduation year)
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing and re-entering information
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if problem continues

### Issue: Cannot delete education entry
**Symptoms**: Delete button doesn't work or entry reappears

**Solution**:
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check that you own the education entry
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if deletion fails

### Issue: Primary education not displaying correctly
**Symptoms**: Wrong education shows as primary on profile

**Solution**:
- Edit the correct entry and set as primary
- System automatically unmarks previous primary
- Refresh your profile page to see changes
- Wait a few seconds for changes to propagate
- Contact support if issue persists

## Additional Notes

### Education Display
- Education entries are sorted by graduation year (most recent first)
- Primary education is highlighted with a badge or special styling
- All education entries appear on your public profile (if profile is public)

### Data Validation
- Program must be selected from dropdown or "Other"
- Campus must be selected from dropdown
- Graduation year must be valid (1970 to current year)
- All fields except major and achievements are required

### Multiple Entries
- You can add unlimited education entries
- Include undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees
- Add certifications and continuing education
- Each entry is displayed separately on your profile

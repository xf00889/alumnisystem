# Mentorship

The mentorship feature connects experienced alumni (mentors) with those seeking guidance (mentees) to foster professional development and career growth.

## Overview

The NORSU Alumni System's mentorship program enables:
- **Mentees**: Find experienced alumni to guide their career development
- **Mentors**: Share knowledge and help fellow alumni succeed
- **Structured Growth**: Track progress through timelines and milestones
- **Communication**: Direct messaging and scheduled meetings
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor achievements and development

## Available Features

### For Mentees

- **[Mentor Search](mentor-search.md)** - Find and connect with mentors
  - Browse available mentors
  - Filter by expertise and availability
  - View detailed mentor profiles
  - Send mentorship requests

- **[Mentorship Requests (Mentee)](mentee-requests.md)** - Manage your mentorship journey
  - Track request status
  - View mentee dashboard
  - Manage active mentorships
  - Schedule meetings with mentors
  - Update progress and milestones

### For Mentors

- **[Mentorship Management (Mentor)](mentor-management.md)** - Guide and support mentees
  - Review and respond to requests
  - Manage mentor dashboard
  - Track mentee progress
  - Schedule and conduct meetings
  - Set timelines and milestones
  - Toggle availability status

## Getting Started

### As a Mentee

1. Complete your profile with your goals and skills
2. Browse available mentors using [Mentor Search](mentor-search.md)
3. Send thoughtful mentorship requests
4. Once accepted, engage actively with your mentor
5. Track your progress and achieve your goals

### As a Mentor

1. Apply for mentor status (if not already approved)
2. Complete your mentor profile with expertise areas
3. Enable "Accepting Mentees" on your dashboard
4. Review and respond to mentorship requests
5. Guide mentees through structured mentorship programs

## Key Benefits

**For Mentees**:
- Gain insights from experienced professionals
- Accelerate career development
- Build professional network
- Learn industry-specific skills
- Receive personalized guidance

**For Mentors**:
- Give back to the alumni community
- Develop leadership skills
- Expand your network
- Stay connected with emerging trends
- Make a meaningful impact

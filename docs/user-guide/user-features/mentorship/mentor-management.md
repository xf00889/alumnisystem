# Mentor Management

This guide explains how to manage your role as a mentor, handle mentorship requests, and use your mentor dashboard effectively.

## Overview

As a mentor, you can review and respond to mentorship requests from mentees, manage your active mentorships, track mentee progress, and schedule meetings. The mentor dashboard provides a centralized view of all your mentoring activities and helps you guide your mentees effectively.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **Required**: Verified mentor account
- **Access Level**: Alumni with approved mentor status
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Completed mentor application and approval
  - Active mentor profile
  - Accepting mentees status enabled
  - Completed profile with expertise areas

## How to Access

### Access Mentor Dashboard

1. Log in to your NORSU Alumni account
2. Click on **"Mentorship"** in the main navigation menu
3. Select **"Mentor Dashboard"**

**Alternative Access**:
- From your main dashboard, click on the mentor widget
- Navigate to **Mentorship > Mentor Dashboard**
- Click on notification links related to mentorship requests

**Expected Result**: Your mentor dashboard loads, showing all your mentoring activities.

![Mentor Dashboard](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-dashboard.png)

## Key Features

- **Review Pending Requests**: Evaluate mentorship requests from mentees
- **Accept or Reject Requests**: Respond to mentorship applications
- **Manage Active Mentorships**: Oversee current mentee relationships
- **View Completed Mentorships**: Review past mentoring experiences
- **Schedule Meetings**: Set up sessions with mentees
- **Track Progress**: Monitor mentee development and achievements
- **Send Messages**: Communicate with mentees
- **Set Timelines**: Create structured mentorship plans
- **Toggle Availability**: Control whether you're accepting new mentees

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Understanding Your Mentor Dashboard

#### Step 1: Dashboard Overview

When you access your mentor dashboard, you'll see several sections:

**Statistics Cards** (Top of page):
- **Active Mentees**: Number of current mentees you're mentoring
- **Pending Requests**: Number of requests awaiting your response
- **Unread Messages**: New messages from mentees
- **Upcoming Meetings**: Number of scheduled meetings

**Availability Toggle**:
- Switch to enable/disable accepting new mentees
- Shows your current availability status
- Affects your visibility in mentor search

**Main Sections**:
1. **Pending Requests**: New mentorship requests to review
2. **Active Mentorships**: Current mentee relationships
3. **Completed Mentorships**: Finished mentorship programs
4. **Upcoming Meetings**: Scheduled sessions with mentees
5. **Recent Progress**: Latest progress updates from mentees

**Expected Result**: You have a complete overview of your mentoring activities.

![Mentor Dashboard Overview](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-dashboard-overview.png)

### Task 2: Managing Your Availability

#### Step 1: Toggle Availability Status

1. Locate the **"Accepting Mentees"** toggle at the top of your dashboard
2. Current status is displayed (e.g., "Currently Accepting Mentees" or "Not Accepting Mentees")

**Expected Result**: You can see your current availability status.

![Availability Toggle](../../screenshots/mentorship/availability-toggle.png)

#### Step 2: Change Availability

1. Click the toggle switch to change your status
2. The system updates your availability immediately

**When Enabled (Accepting Mentees)**:
- Your profile appears in mentor search results
- Mentees can send you requests
- You're marked as "Available" or "Limited"
- You receive request notifications

**When Disabled (Not Accepting Mentees)**:
- Your profile is hidden from mentor search
- Mentees cannot send new requests
- Existing mentorships continue normally
- You can still manage current mentees

**Expected Result**: 
- Toggle switches to new state
- Confirmation message appears
- Your search visibility updates

**When to Disable**:
- You've reached your mentee capacity
- You need a break from mentoring
- You're too busy with other commitments
- You're going on leave or vacation

**When to Enable**:
- You have capacity for new mentees
- You want to help more alumni
- You've completed some mentorships
- You're ready to take on new challenges

### Task 3: Reviewing and Responding to Requests

#### Step 1: View Pending Requests

1. Scroll to the **"Pending Requests"** section
2. This shows all mentorship requests awaiting your response

**Expected Result**: You see cards for each pending request.

![Pending Requests](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-pending-requests.png)

#### Step 2: Review Request Details

Each request card displays:

**Mentee Information**:
- Name and profile photo
- Current position or student status
- Education background
- Location

**Request Details**:
- **Skills Seeking**: What they want to learn
- **Career Goals**: Their professional objectives
- **Message**: Why they chose you as a mentor
- **Date Submitted**: When request was sent

**Mentee Profile Link**:
- Click to view full profile
- Review their experience, education, skills
- Check for alignment with your expertise

**Expected Result**: You have all information needed to make a decision.

![Request Details](../../screenshots/mentorship/request-details.png)

#### Step 3: Review Mentee Profile

Before accepting:

1. Click **"View Profile"** on the request card
2. Review mentee's:
   - Professional background
   - Education history
   - Skills and interests
   - Career goals
   - Previous achievements

3. Assess compatibility:
   - Do their goals align with your expertise?
   - Can you help them achieve their objectives?
   - Do you have time to commit?
   - Is there a good personality fit?

**Expected Result**: You can make an informed decision about the request.

#### Step 4: Accept a Request

If you want to mentor this person:

1. On the request card, click **"Accept"** or **"Approve"** button
2. A confirmation dialog may appear
3. Confirm your acceptance

**Expected Result**: 
- Request is approved
- Mentee receives acceptance notification
- Mentorship moves to "Active Mentorships"
- Your active mentee count increases
- You can now message and schedule meetings

![Accept Request](../../screenshots/mentorship/accept-request.png)

**After Accepting**:
1. Send a welcome message to your mentee
2. Schedule an introductory meeting
3. Discuss goals and expectations
4. Set up a mentorship timeline
5. Establish communication frequency

#### Step 5: Reject a Request

If you cannot mentor this person:

1. On the request card, click **"Reject"** or **"Decline"** button
2. Optionally provide a reason (if prompted)
3. Confirm your decision

**Expected Result**: 
- Request is declined
- Mentee receives rejection notification
- Request is removed from pending list
- Mentee can request other mentors

![Reject Request](../../screenshots/mentorship/reject-request.png)

**Valid Reasons to Reject**:
- At full mentee capacity
- Goals don't align with your expertise
- Time constraints
- Geographic or timezone issues
- Better mentor available for their needs
- Conflict of interest

**Rejection Best Practices**:
- Be respectful and professional
- Provide constructive feedback if possible
- Suggest alternative mentors if appropriate
- Don't take too long to respond

### Task 4: Managing Active Mentorships

#### Step 1: View Active Mentorships

1. Scroll to the **"Active Mentorships"** section
2. This shows all your current mentee relationships

**Expected Result**: You see cards for each active mentorship.

![Active Mentorships](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-active-mentorships.png)

#### Step 2: Review Mentorship Details

Each active mentorship card includes:

**Mentee Information**:
- Name and profile photo
- Current position
- Contact information

**Mentorship Details**:
- Start date
- Duration or expected end date
- Progress percentage
- Current status (Active, Paused)
- Timeline milestones (if set)

**Recent Activity**:
- Last meeting date
- Last message date
- Recent progress updates

**Quick Actions**:
- **Send Message**: Open messaging interface
- **Schedule Meeting**: Create a new meeting
- **View Progress**: See detailed progress
- **Add Progress Note**: Record mentee achievements
- **Update Status**: Change mentorship status
- **Set Timeline**: Create or update milestones

**Expected Result**: You have complete visibility into each mentorship.

#### Step 3: Send a Message to Mentee

1. On the mentorship card, click **"Send Message"**
2. A message modal or page opens
3. Type your message
4. Optionally attach files
5. Click **"Send"**

**Expected Result**: 
- Message is sent to mentee
- Mentee receives notification
- Message appears in conversation history

![Send Message to Mentee](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-send-message.png)

**Message Tips**:
- Check in regularly (weekly or bi-weekly)
- Provide constructive feedback
- Share relevant resources
- Encourage and motivate
- Ask about challenges
- Celebrate achievements

#### Step 4: Schedule a Meeting with Mentee

1. Click **"Schedule Meeting"** on the mentorship card
2. Fill in meeting details:
   - **Title**: Meeting purpose (e.g., "Monthly Check-in", "Goal Review")
   - **Description**: Agenda or topics to discuss
   - **Date and Time**: When meeting will occur
   - **Duration**: Length in minutes (typically 30-60)
   - **Meeting Link**: Video call URL or physical location

3. Click **"Schedule"** or **"Create Meeting"**

**Expected Result**: 
- Meeting is created
- Mentee receives notification
- Meeting appears in upcoming meetings
- Calendar invite sent (if configured)

![Schedule Meeting with Mentee](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-schedule-meeting.png)

**Meeting Best Practices**:
- Schedule regular meetings (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
- Provide clear agenda in advance
- Set up recurring meetings if appropriate
- Include video call link
- Send reminder before meeting
- Keep meetings focused and productive

#### Step 5: Track and Update Mentee Progress

1. Click **"View Progress"** or **"Add Progress Note"**
2. Review existing progress updates
3. To add new progress:
   - **Title**: Brief description of achievement
   - **Description**: Detailed progress notes
   - **Completed Items**: What mentee accomplished
   - **Next Steps**: Upcoming goals or tasks

4. Optionally update progress percentage
5. Click **"Save"** or **"Submit"**

**Expected Result**: 
- Progress update is saved
- Mentee can see your notes
- Progress percentage updates
- Timeline reflects progress

![Track Progress](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-track-progress.png)

**What to Track**:
- Skills developed
- Goals achieved
- Challenges overcome
- Projects completed
- Behavioral improvements
- Career milestones reached
- Confidence growth

#### Step 6: Set or Update Timeline

1. On the mentorship card, click **"Set Timeline"** or **"Update Timeline"**
2. Fill in timeline details:
   - **Expected End Date**: When mentorship should complete
   - **Milestones**: List of goals with timeframes

3. Add milestones:
   - **Period**: Week/Day range (e.g., "Week 1-2", "Week 3-4")
   - **Description**: What should be achieved
   - Click **"Add Milestone"** for more

4. Add notes about the timeline
5. Click **"Save Timeline"**

**Expected Result**: 
- Timeline is set for mentorship
- Milestones appear on dashboard
- Both you and mentee can track progress
- Progress percentage calculates based on milestones

![Set Timeline](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-set-timeline.png)

**Timeline Best Practices**:
- Set realistic, achievable milestones
- Break large goals into smaller steps
- Align with mentee's goals
- Review and adjust as needed
- Celebrate milestone completions
- Use milestones to structure meetings

#### Step 7: Update Mentorship Status

1. Click **"Update Status"** or status dropdown
2. Select new status:
   - **Active**: Ongoing mentorship
   - **Paused**: Temporarily on hold
   - **Completed**: Successfully finished
   - **Cancelled**: Ended early

3. Provide reason for status change (if required)
4. Confirm the change

**Expected Result**: 
- Status updates
- Mentee receives notification
- Mentorship moves to appropriate section
- Your active mentee count adjusts

![Update Status](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-update-status.png)

**When to Change Status**:

**Pause**:
- Temporary break needed
- Mentee or mentor unavailable
- External circumstances
- Plan to resume later

**Complete**:
- Goals achieved
- Timeline finished
- Mutual agreement to end
- Successful mentorship

**Cancel**:
- Incompatibility issues
- Lack of engagement
- Changed circumstances
- Mutual decision to end early

### Task 5: Managing Meetings

#### Step 1: View Upcoming Meetings

1. Locate the **"Upcoming Meetings"** section
2. This shows all scheduled meetings with mentees

![Upcoming Meetings](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-upcoming-meetings.png)

#### Step 2: Review Meeting Details

Each meeting card displays:

- **Meeting Title**: Purpose of the meeting
- **Mentee Name**: Who you're meeting with
- **Date and Time**: When meeting occurs
- **Duration**: Length of meeting
- **Meeting Link**: Video call or location URL
- **Description**: Agenda or notes
- **Status**: Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled, Rescheduled

**Actions**:
- **Join Meeting**: Access meeting link
- **View Details**: See full information
- **Edit Meeting**: Modify details
- **Cancel Meeting**: Cancel if needed
- **Mark Complete**: Update status after meeting

**Expected Result**: You're prepared for all meetings.

#### Step 3: Conduct and Complete Meeting

1. Join meeting at scheduled time
2. Follow your agenda
3. Take notes during meeting
4. After meeting, click **"Mark Complete"**
5. Optionally add meeting notes or outcomes

**Expected Result**: 
- Meeting status updates to "Completed"
- Notes are saved
- Both parties can reference meeting history

**Meeting Agenda Suggestions**:
- Review progress since last meeting
- Discuss challenges and solutions
- Set goals for next period
- Answer mentee questions
- Provide feedback and guidance
- Assign action items
- Schedule next meeting

## Tips and Best Practices

### Effective Mentor Practices

1. **Be Responsive**
   - Reply to messages within 24-48 hours
   - Acknowledge progress updates promptly
   - Keep scheduled meetings
   - Be available for urgent questions

2. **Set Clear Expectations**
   - Discuss goals in first meeting
   - Establish communication frequency
   - Define meeting schedule
   - Set boundaries and availability

3. **Provide Constructive Feedback**
   - Be honest but supportive
   - Focus on growth areas
   - Celebrate achievements
   - Offer specific, actionable advice

4. **Share Your Experience**
   - Tell relevant stories
   - Share lessons learned
   - Provide industry insights
   - Connect mentees with your network

5. **Track Progress Systematically**
   - Update progress regularly
   - Review milestones together
   - Adjust goals as needed
   - Document achievements

6. **Maintain Professionalism**
   - Keep appropriate boundaries
   - Respect confidentiality
   - Be reliable and consistent
   - Model professional behavior

### Dashboard Management Tips

1. **Check Regularly**: Review dashboard at least 2-3 times per week
2. **Respond Promptly**: Address pending requests within 3-5 days
3. **Update Progress**: Add notes after each meeting or milestone
4. **Monitor Capacity**: Don't overcommit; quality over quantity
5. **Stay Organized**: Use dashboard sections to manage activities
6. **Set Reminders**: Use calendar for meetings and check-ins

### Request Evaluation Criteria

**Good Indicators**:
- Clear, specific goals
- Thoughtful request message
- Alignment with your expertise
- Realistic expectations
- Complete profile
- Genuine interest

**Red Flags**:
- Vague or generic requests
- Unrealistic expectations
- No clear goals
- Incomplete profile
- Misaligned expertise
- Too many concurrent requests

### Mentorship Capacity Guidelines

**Recommended Limits**:
- **New Mentors**: 1-2 mentees to start
- **Experienced Mentors**: 3-5 mentees maximum
- **Senior Mentors**: Up to 7-8 mentees if very experienced

**Factors to Consider**:
- Your available time
- Mentee needs and goals
- Meeting frequency
- Other commitments
- Energy and capacity
- Quality of mentoring

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: First-Time Mentor
**Scenario**: You're new to mentoring

**Steps**:
1. Start with 1-2 mentees
2. Review requests carefully
3. Set clear expectations upfront
4. Schedule regular meetings
5. Ask for feedback from mentees
6. Learn and adjust your approach

### Use Case 2: Managing Multiple Mentees
**Scenario**: You have several active mentorships

**Steps**:
1. Use dashboard to stay organized
2. Set different meeting schedules
3. Track each mentee's progress separately
4. Use templates for common advice
5. Group similar topics when possible
6. Don't overcommit

### Use Case 3: Specialized Mentoring
**Scenario**: Mentoring in specific technical area

**Steps**:
1. Accept requests aligned with expertise
2. Create structured learning paths
3. Share technical resources
4. Review mentee's work/projects
5. Provide hands-on guidance
6. Connect with industry contacts

### Use Case 4: Career Coaching
**Scenario**: Helping mentees with career transitions

**Steps**:
1. Assess current situation and goals
2. Create career development timeline
3. Review resumes and materials
4. Conduct mock interviews
5. Share job opportunities
6. Provide networking introductions

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Access Mentor Dashboard

**Problem**: Dashboard shows error or won't load

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you have mentor status
2. Check if mentor profile is active
3. Refresh the page
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try different browser
6. Contact support if issue persists

### Requests Not Appearing

**Problem**: No pending requests showing

**Possible Reasons**:
1. No new requests received
2. Availability is disabled
3. Profile not visible in search
4. At mentee capacity
5. Expertise areas not set

**Solutions**:
1. Enable "Accepting Mentees" toggle
2. Update your mentor profile
3. Add expertise areas
4. Check mentor application status
5. Increase mentee capacity

### Cannot Accept/Reject Request

**Problem**: Action buttons don't work

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify you're logged in
4. Try different browser
5. Clear browser cache
6. Contact support

### Meeting Scheduling Issues

**Problem**: Cannot create meetings

**Solutions**:
1. Verify mentorship is active
2. Check all required fields
3. Ensure date is in future
4. Verify meeting link format
5. Try again after refreshing

### Progress Not Updating

**Problem**: Progress updates don't save

**Solutions**:
1. Check all required fields
2. Ensure description length is valid
3. Verify internet connection
4. Refresh and try again
5. Use quick progress update

### Availability Toggle Not Working

**Problem**: Cannot change availability status

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check if you're at capacity
3. Verify mentor profile is active
4. Try logging out and back in
5. Contact support if persists

## Privacy and Security

### Your Information

- **Dashboard Privacy**: Only you see your full dashboard
- **Mentee Access**: Mentees see only their mentorship with you
- **Progress Visibility**: Only you and specific mentee see their progress
- **Message Privacy**: Messages are private between you and mentee
- **Request Privacy**: Only you see pending requests

### Data Security

- All communications are encrypted
- Progress data is securely stored
- Meeting links are private
- Mentee information is confidential
- Account activity is logged

### Professional Boundaries

- Maintain appropriate mentor-mentee relationship
- Keep communications professional
- Respect confidentiality
- Don't share mentee information
- Report any inappropriate behavior

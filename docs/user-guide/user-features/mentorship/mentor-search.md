# Mentor Search

This guide explains how to search for and find mentors who can guide you in your career development.

## Overview

The mentor search feature allows you to discover experienced alumni who are willing to share their knowledge and provide guidance. You can filter mentors by expertise, availability, and experience level to find the perfect match for your career goals.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **Required**: Authenticated alumni account
- **Access Level**: All registered alumni users (mentees)
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Completed profile setup
  - Verified email address
  - Active account status

## How to Access

1. Log in to your NORSU Alumni account
2. Click on **"Mentorship"** in the main navigation menu
3. You'll be directed to the Mentor Search page

**Alternative Access**:
- From your dashboard, look for the "Find a Mentor" quick link
- Navigate to **Mentorship > Find Mentors**

**Expected Result**: The Mentor Search page loads, displaying available mentors who are currently accepting mentees.

![Mentor Search Page](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-search-main.png)

## Key Features

- **Browse Available Mentors**: View all mentors currently accepting mentees
- **Filter by Expertise**: Find mentors with specific skills or knowledge areas
- **Filter by Availability**: See mentors based on their availability status
- **Filter by Experience**: Find mentors with substantial mentoring experience
- **Sort Options**: Organize results by experience, availability, or most recent
- **View Mentor Profiles**: See detailed information about each mentor
- **Direct Request**: Send mentorship requests directly from search results

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Browse Available Mentors

#### Step 1: View the Mentor List

1. Navigate to the Mentor Search page
2. The page displays mentor cards in a grid layout
3. Each card shows:
   - Mentor's name and photo
   - Current position and company
   - Areas of expertise
   - Availability status
   - Years of experience
   - Number of current mentees

**Expected Result**: You see a list of mentors who are actively accepting new mentees.

![Mentor Cards Display](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-cards.png)

#### Step 2: Review Mentor Information

On each mentor card, you'll find:

- **Profile Photo**: Visual identification
- **Name**: Full name of the mentor
- **Professional Title**: Current job position
- **Expertise Areas**: Skills and knowledge domains (e.g., "Software Development, Project Management, Career Coaching")
- **Availability Badge**: 
  - 游릭 **Available**: Actively accepting mentees
  - 游리 **Limited**: Limited availability
  - 游댮 **Unavailable**: Not accepting new mentees
- **Experience Indicator**: Shows mentoring experience level
- **Current Mentees**: Number of active mentees (if displayed)
- **Action Buttons**: "View Profile" and "Request Mentorship"

**Expected Result**: You can quickly assess each mentor's qualifications and availability.

### Task 2: Filter Mentors by Expertise

#### Step 1: Access Expertise Filter

1. Locate the **"Filter by Expertise"** section on the left sidebar or top of the page
2. You'll see a search input or dropdown for expertise areas

![Expertise Filter](../../screenshots/mentorship/expertise-filter.png)

#### Step 2: Enter Expertise Keywords

1. Type keywords related to the skills you want to develop
   - Examples: "Software Development", "Marketing", "Data Science", "Leadership"
2. You can enter multiple expertise areas separated by commas
3. The system searches for mentors whose expertise matches your keywords

**Expected Result**: The mentor list updates to show only mentors with matching expertise.

#### Step 3: Apply the Filter

1. Click **"Apply"** or **"Search"** button
2. The page refreshes with filtered results

**Expected Result**: You see mentors who specialize in your areas of interest.

**Tips**:
- Use specific terms for better results (e.g., "Python Programming" instead of just "Programming")
- Try variations if you don't find matches (e.g., "Web Development" vs "Frontend Development")
- Combine multiple expertise areas to find versatile mentors

### Task 3: Filter by Availability Status

#### Step 1: Locate Availability Filter

1. Find the **"Availability"** filter section
2. You'll see options like:
   - **Available**: Actively accepting mentees
   - **Limited**: Limited availability
   - **All**: Show all mentors regardless of availability

![Availability Filter](../../screenshots/mentorship/availability-filter.png)

#### Step 2: Select Availability Option

1. Click on your preferred availability status
2. The default is usually "Available" to show only mentors accepting mentees

**Expected Result**: The list updates to show mentors matching your availability preference.

**Note**: Mentors marked as "Unavailable" typically won't appear in search results unless you specifically select "All".

### Task 4: Filter by Experience Level

#### Step 1: Access Experience Filter

1. Look for the **"Experience Level"** or **"Experienced Mentors"** filter
2. You may see a checkbox or toggle option

![Experience Filter](../../screenshots/mentorship/experience-filter.png)

#### Step 2: Enable Experience Filter

1. Check the box for **"Show only experienced mentors"**
2. This filters mentors who have:
   - Substantial mentoring experience
   - Completed multiple mentorships
   - Positive mentoring history

**Expected Result**: The list shows only mentors with proven mentoring experience.

**When to Use**:
- You want guidance from seasoned mentors
- You're working on complex career challenges
- You prefer mentors with a track record

### Task 5: Sort Mentor Results

#### Step 1: Locate Sort Options

1. Find the **"Sort by"** dropdown menu (usually at the top right of the results)
2. Available sort options typically include:
   - **Experience**: Mentors with most experience first
   - **Availability**: Most available mentors first
   - **Most Recent**: Newest mentors first

![Sort Options](../../screenshots/mentorship/sort-options.png)

#### Step 2: Select Sort Preference

1. Click on the dropdown menu
2. Select your preferred sorting method
3. The list automatically reorders

**Expected Result**: Mentors are displayed in your chosen order.

**Sort Recommendations**:
- **Experience**: Best for finding established mentors
- **Availability**: Best when you need immediate mentorship
- **Most Recent**: Best for discovering new mentors

### Task 6: View Detailed Mentor Profile

#### Step 1: Access Mentor Profile

1. On any mentor card, click the **"View Profile"** button
2. Or click on the mentor's name or photo

**Expected Result**: You're taken to the mentor's full profile page.

![Mentor Profile Button](../../screenshots/mentorship/view-profile-button.png)

#### Step 2: Review Profile Details

The mentor profile page includes:

**Professional Information**:
- Full name and profile photo
- Current position and company
- Professional summary or bio
- Years of professional experience

**Expertise and Skills**:
- List of expertise areas
- Technical and soft skills
- Industry knowledge
- Certifications (if listed)

**Mentoring Information**:
- Mentoring experience description
- Areas they can help with
- Mentoring style or approach
- Availability status
- Current number of mentees
- Maximum mentees capacity

**Education Background**:
- Degrees and institutions
- Graduation years
- Academic achievements

**Work Experience**:
- Current and past positions
- Companies and organizations
- Career progression
- Notable achievements

**Mentorship Statistics** (if available):
- Number of mentees mentored
- Completed mentorships
- Average mentorship duration
- Mentee success stories or testimonials

**Expected Result**: You have comprehensive information to decide if this mentor is right for you.

![Full Mentor Profile](../../screenshots/mentorship/mentor-profile-full.png)

#### Step 3: Assess Compatibility

Consider these factors:
- **Expertise Match**: Do their skills align with your learning goals?
- **Experience Level**: Do they have the depth of experience you need?
- **Availability**: Can they commit the time you need?
- **Industry**: Are they in your target industry or field?
- **Career Stage**: Have they achieved what you aspire to?
- **Communication Style**: Does their profile suggest a good fit?

### Task 7: Request Mentorship from Search Results

#### Step 1: Initiate Request from Card

1. On the mentor card, click the **"Request Mentorship"** button
2. Or from the full profile page, click **"Request Mentorship"**

**Expected Result**: You're taken to the mentorship request form.

![Request Mentorship Button](../../screenshots/mentorship/request-button.png)

#### Step 2: Complete the Request Form

The mentorship request form includes:

1. **Skills You're Seeking** (Required)
   - Describe what skills you want to develop
   - Be specific about your learning objectives
   - Example: "I want to improve my Python programming skills and learn about machine learning applications"

2. **Your Career Goals** (Required)
   - Explain your short-term and long-term career goals
   - How mentorship fits into your career plan
   - Example: "I aim to transition from web development to data science within the next year"

3. **Why This Mentor** (Required)
   - Explain why you chose this specific mentor
   - What about their background appeals to you
   - How you think they can help you
   - Example: "Your experience in leading data science teams at tech companies aligns perfectly with my career aspirations"

4. **Security Verification** (if enabled)
   - Complete reCAPTCHA verification

![Mentorship Request Form](../../screenshots/mentorship/request-form.png)

#### Step 3: Submit the Request

1. Review all your responses
2. Ensure your message is professional and thoughtful
3. Click **"Submit Request"** or **"Send Request"**

**Expected Result**: 
- Success message: "Your mentorship request has been submitted successfully and is awaiting approval from the mentor"
- You're redirected to the mentor search page or your mentee dashboard
- The mentor receives a notification about your request
- The request appears in your "Pending Requests" list

![Request Submitted](../../screenshots/mentorship/request-submitted.png)

**Important Notes**:
- You can only have one pending request per mentor
- Requests don't expire automatically
- The mentor will review and respond to your request
- You'll receive a notification when they respond

## Understanding Mentor Availability

### Availability Statuses

1. **游릭 Available**
   - Actively accepting new mentees
   - Has capacity for more mentees
   - Likely to respond quickly
   - Best time to send requests

2. **游리 Limited Availability**
   - May accept select mentees
   - Near capacity
   - May have specific criteria
   - Response time may vary

3. **游댮 Unavailable**
   - Not accepting new mentees
   - At full capacity
   - May be on hiatus
   - Cannot send requests

### Mentor Capacity

- Mentors set their own capacity limits
- Most mentors accept 2-5 mentees at a time
- Popular mentors may reach capacity quickly
- Check back regularly for availability changes

## Tips for Finding the Right Mentor

### Best Practices

1. **Define Your Goals First**
   - Know what you want to achieve
   - Identify specific skills to develop
   - Understand your career direction
   - This helps you find the right match

2. **Research Thoroughly**
   - Read full profiles carefully
   - Check their expertise areas
   - Review their career progression
   - Look for alignment with your goals

3. **Consider Multiple Factors**
   - Don't focus only on job titles
   - Consider industry experience
   - Look at mentoring experience
   - Assess availability and commitment

4. **Be Realistic**
   - Popular mentors may be at capacity
   - Consider less obvious matches
   - Look for rising professionals
   - Quality over prestige

5. **Prepare Before Requesting**
   - Complete your own profile first
   - Have clear goals in mind
   - Prepare thoughtful request messages
   - Show genuine interest

### What Makes a Good Mentor Match

**Strong Indicators**:
- Expertise in your target area
- Career path you want to follow
- Industry experience you need
- Available and responsive
- Proven mentoring experience
- Shared values or interests
- Good communication style

**Consider Carefully**:
- Very different career paths
- No relevant expertise
- Unavailable or overcommitted
- No mentoring experience
- Misaligned goals or values

### Who to Look For

**Ideal Mentor Candidates**:
- Alumni 5-15 years ahead in career
- Professionals in your target industry
- Experts in skills you want to develop
- People with diverse experiences
- Those with mentoring experience
- Alumni with similar backgrounds
- Professionals in your geographic area (for in-person meetings)

## Troubleshooting

### No Mentors Found

**Problem**: Search returns no results

**Solutions**:
1. **Broaden Your Search**:
   - Remove or reduce filters
   - Try more general expertise terms
   - Select "All" for availability

2. **Check Spelling**:
   - Verify expertise keywords are spelled correctly
   - Try alternative terms or synonyms

3. **Try Different Keywords**:
   - Use broader categories
   - Example: "Technology" instead of "React.js"

4. **Check Back Later**:
   - Mentor availability changes
   - New mentors join regularly
   - Set up alerts if available

### Mentor Profile Won't Load

**Problem**: Clicking "View Profile" doesn't work

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily
6. Contact support if issue persists

### Filter Not Working

**Problem**: Applying filters doesn't change results

**Solutions**:
1. Click "Apply" or "Search" button explicitly
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Clear all filters and reapply
4. Check if JavaScript is enabled
5. Try a different browser

### Cannot Send Request

**Problem**: "Request Mentorship" button is disabled or not working

**Possible Reasons**:
1. **Already Sent Request**: You have a pending request with this mentor
2. **Mentor Unavailable**: Mentor is not accepting mentees
3. **Already Connected**: You're already in a mentorship with them
4. **Account Issues**: Your account may need verification

**Solutions**:
1. Check your pending requests list
2. Verify mentor's availability status
3. Complete your profile if incomplete
4. Verify your email address
5. Contact support if none apply

### Search Results Not Updating

**Problem**: Results don't change after applying filters

**Solutions**:
1. Wait a few seconds for processing
2. Refresh the page manually
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check internet connection
5. Try logging out and back in

## Privacy and Security

### What Mentors See

When you view a mentor's profile:
- Your view is not tracked or notified
- Mentors don't know who viewed their profile
- Your browsing is private

When you send a request:
- Mentor sees your full profile
- They see your request message
- They can review your background
- Your contact information is shared

### Your Information

- **Profile Visibility**: Your profile is visible to all mentors
- **Request Privacy**: Only the mentor sees your request
- **No Public Requests**: Other users don't see your pending requests
- **Secure Communication**: All interactions are private

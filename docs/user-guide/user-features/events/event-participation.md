# Event Participation

## Overview

Event Participation allows you to engage with alumni events by viewing detailed event information, responding to event invitations (RSVP), managing your attendance status, and tracking events you plan to attend. This feature helps you stay connected with the alumni community and participate in meaningful activities.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **User Role**: All authenticated alumni users
- **Permissions Required**: Active user account
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Must be logged in to the system
  - Events must be published and available

## How to Access

1. Log in to your alumni account
2. Navigate to **"Events"** in the main navigation menu
3. Click on any event to view its details and participation options

## Key Features

- View comprehensive event details
- RSVP to events with three status options (Attending, Not Attending, Maybe)
- Add optional notes with your RSVP
- Change your RSVP status at any time before the event
- View RSVP counts to see how many people are attending
- Access virtual event links for online events
- Track all your events in one place (My Events)
- View upcoming and past events you've RSVP'd to

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Viewing Event Details

Access comprehensive information about a specific event.

1. **Navigate to the Events List**
   - Go to **Events** from the main navigation menu
   - **Expected Result**: The events list is displayed

2. **Select an event**
   - Click on any event card or event title
   - **Expected Result**: The event detail page opens

3. **Review event information**
   - The event detail page displays:
     - **Event title** and description
     - **Date and time**: Start and end date/time
     - **Location**: Physical address or "Virtual Event"
     - **Virtual link**: For online events (if available)
     - **Event status**: Published, Cancelled, Completed
     - **Visibility**: Public or Private
     - **Max participants**: Capacity limit (if set)
     - **Event image**: Visual representation of the event
     - **Created by**: Event organizer information
     - **RSVP counts**: Number of people attending, not attending, or maybe
   - **Expected Result**: You have all the information needed to decide about attending

4. **Check event timing**
   - Note whether the event is:
     - **Upcoming**: Scheduled for the future
     - **Ongoing**: Currently happening
     - **Past**: Already completed
   - **Expected Result**: You understand the event's timing status

### Task 2: RSVP to an Event

Respond to an event invitation and indicate your attendance status.

1. **Open the event detail page**
   - Navigate to **Events** and click on an event
   - **Expected Result**: Event details are displayed

2. **Locate the RSVP form**
   - Scroll to the RSVP section on the event detail page
   - You'll see the RSVP form with status options
   - **Expected Result**: The RSVP form is visible

3. **Select your RSVP status**
   - Choose one of the following options:
     - **Attending (Yes)**: You plan to attend the event
     - **Not Attending (No)**: You cannot attend the event
     - **Maybe**: You're unsure about your attendance
   - Click on your preferred status
   - **Expected Result**: Your selection is highlighted

4. **Add optional notes (optional)**
   - In the notes field, you can add:
     - Dietary restrictions
     - Special requirements
     - Questions for organizers
     - Any other relevant information
   - **Expected Result**: Your notes are entered in the text field

5. **Submit your RSVP**
   - Click the **"Submit RSVP"** or **"Save"** button
   - **Expected Result**: A success message appears confirming your RSVP has been recorded

6. **Verify your RSVP**
   - Your RSVP status should now be displayed on the event page
   - The RSVP counts should update to reflect your response
   - **Expected Result**: Your attendance status is saved and visible

### Task 3: Changing Your RSVP Status

Update your attendance status if your plans change.

1. **Navigate to the event**
   - Go to **Events** and find the event you RSVP'd to
   - Click on the event to open its detail page
   - **Expected Result**: Event details are displayed with your current RSVP

2. **View your current RSVP**
   - Your existing RSVP status is displayed in the RSVP form
   - It may be highlighted or pre-selected
   - **Expected Result**: You can see your current attendance status

3. **Select a new status**
   - Choose a different RSVP option:
     - Change from "Attending" to "Maybe" or "Not Attending"
     - Change from "Maybe" to "Attending" or "Not Attending"
     - Change from "Not Attending" to "Attending" or "Maybe"
   - **Expected Result**: The new status is selected

4. **Update your notes (optional)**
   - Modify or add notes if needed
   - Explain why you're changing your status (optional)
   - **Expected Result**: Updated notes are entered

5. **Save your changes**
   - Click the **"Submit RSVP"** or **"Update"** button
   - **Expected Result**: A success message confirms your RSVP has been updated

6. **Confirm the update**
   - The event page should reflect your new RSVP status
   - RSVP counts are updated accordingly
   - **Expected Result**: Your new attendance status is saved

### Task 4: Viewing My Events

Access a personalized list of events you've RSVP'd to.

1. **Navigate to My Events**
   - From the Events page, look for a **"My Events"** link or button
   - Alternatively, access it from your user menu or dashboard
   - Click on **"My Events"**
   - **Expected Result**: Your personalized events page opens

2. **Review upcoming events**
   - The "Upcoming Events" section shows events you've RSVP'd to that haven't occurred yet
   - Events are listed with:
     - Event title
     - Date and time
     - Your RSVP status
     - Location
   - **Expected Result**: You see all future events you're involved with

3. **Review past events**
   - The "Past Events" section shows events you RSVP'd to that have already occurred
   - This helps you track your event participation history
   - **Expected Result**: You see your event attendance history

4. **Access event details from My Events**
   - Click on any event in your list to view full details
   - You can update your RSVP from the event detail page
   - **Expected Result**: Event details open for further action

### Task 5: Accessing Virtual Event Links

Join online events using virtual meeting links.

1. **Find a virtual event**
   - Browse events and identify virtual events (marked as "Virtual Event")
   - Click on the virtual event to view details
   - **Expected Result**: Virtual event details are displayed

2. **Locate the virtual link**
   - On the event detail page, look for the "Virtual Link" or "Join Online" section
   - The link may be displayed as:
     - A clickable URL
     - A "Join Meeting" button
     - A Zoom/Teams/Google Meet link
   - **Expected Result**: The virtual meeting link is visible

3. **RSVP to the virtual event**
   - Submit your RSVP as "Attending" to confirm your participation
   - **Expected Result**: Your RSVP is recorded

4. **Join the event**
   - When the event time arrives, return to the event detail page
   - Click on the virtual link to join the meeting
   - **Expected Result**: You're redirected to the virtual meeting platform

5. **Prepare for the virtual event**
   - Test your internet connection beforehand
   - Ensure you have the necessary software (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
   - Join a few minutes early to resolve any technical issues
   - **Expected Result**: You're ready to participate in the virtual event

### Task 6: Viewing RSVP Counts

See how many people are attending an event.

1. **Open an event detail page**
   - Navigate to **Events** and click on any event
   - **Expected Result**: Event details are displayed

2. **Locate the RSVP counts section**
   - Look for a section showing attendance statistics
   - You'll see counts for:
     - **Attending**: Number of people who selected "Yes"
     - **Not Attending**: Number of people who selected "No"
     - **Maybe**: Number of people who are unsure
   - **Expected Result**: RSVP statistics are visible

3. **Check event capacity**
   - If the event has a maximum participant limit, it will be displayed
   - Compare the "Attending" count with the max participants
   - **Expected Result**: You know if the event is near capacity

4. **Make informed decisions**
   - Use RSVP counts to gauge event popularity
   - Consider capacity when deciding to attend
   - **Expected Result**: You have information to help plan your attendance

## Tips and Best Practices

- **RSVP early**: Respond to event invitations as soon as possible, especially for events with limited capacity
- **Update your status**: If your plans change, update your RSVP promptly so organizers can plan accordingly
- **Add meaningful notes**: Use the notes field to communicate important information to event organizers
- **Check My Events regularly**: Keep track of your upcoming events to avoid missing important activities
- **Virtual event preparation**: For virtual events, test the meeting link before the event starts
- **Respect capacity limits**: If an event is at capacity, consider selecting "Maybe" or "Not Attending" to allow others to participate
- **Review event details carefully**: Check the date, time, and location before RSVP'ing to avoid conflicts
- **Set reminders**: Add events to your personal calendar to ensure you don't forget

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Attending a Reunion Event
Browse upcoming events, find your class reunion, RSVP as "Attending," and add notes about dietary restrictions. Check My Events regularly to stay updated.

### Use Case 2: Tentative Attendance
You're interested in an event but unsure about your schedule. RSVP as "Maybe" initially, then update to "Attending" or "Not Attending" once your schedule is confirmed.

### Use Case 3: Joining a Virtual Workshop
Find a virtual career workshop, RSVP as "Attending," note the virtual link, and join the meeting when the event starts.

### Use Case 4: Cancelling Your Attendance
You RSVP'd to an event but can no longer attend. Update your RSVP to "Not Attending" and add a note explaining your absence.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot Submit RSVP
**Symptoms**: The RSVP form doesn't submit or shows an error
**Solution**:
- Ensure you've selected an RSVP status (Attending, Not Attending, or Maybe)
- Check that you're logged in to your account
- Verify your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and submitting again
- If the issue persists, contact system administrators

### Issue: RSVP Status Not Updating
**Symptoms**: Your RSVP change doesn't save or revert to the old status
**Solution**:
- Ensure you clicked the "Submit" or "Update" button after making changes
- Check for any error messages on the page
- Refresh the page to see if the update was actually saved
- Try using a different browser
- Contact support if the problem continues

### Issue: Virtual Link Not Visible
**Symptoms**: Can't find the virtual meeting link for an online event
**Solution**:
- Ensure you've RSVP'd to the event (some links may only be visible to attendees)
- Check if the event organizer has added the link yet (it may be added closer to the event date)
- Refresh the page to load the latest information
- Contact the event organizer if the link is still missing

### Issue: My Events Page is Empty
**Symptoms**: No events appear in your My Events list
**Solution**:
- Ensure you've RSVP'd to at least one event
- Check if you're logged in to the correct account
- Try browsing events and RSVP'ing to a new event
- Refresh the page
- Contact support if events you've RSVP'd to don't appear

### Issue: Event is at Capacity
**Symptoms**: Cannot RSVP as "Attending" because the event is full
**Solution**:
- Check if the event has a waitlist option
- RSVP as "Maybe" to express interest
- Contact the event organizer to inquire about additional capacity
- Look for similar events that may have availability

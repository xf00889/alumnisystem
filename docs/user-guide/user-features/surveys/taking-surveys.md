# Taking Surveys

## Overview

Taking surveys is an important way to contribute to the alumni community. Your responses help administrators understand alumni needs, track career outcomes, gather feedback, and make data-driven decisions. The system supports various question types and makes it easy to complete surveys at your convenience.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **User Role**: All authenticated alumni users
- **Permissions Required**: Active user account
- **Prerequisites**: 
  - Must be logged in to the system
  - Must have an active alumni profile
  - Survey must be in "Active" status

## How to Access

1. Log in to your alumni account
2. Click on **"Surveys"** in the main navigation menu
3. You will be directed to the Surveys List page showing all active surveys

## Key Features

- View all active surveys available for completion
- See which surveys you've already completed
- Complete surveys with various question types
- Access external surveys (Google Forms, etc.)
- Receive immediate confirmation after submission
- One-time submission per survey (prevents duplicate responses)

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Accessing Available Surveys

View all surveys that are currently available for you to complete.

1. **Navigate to the Surveys page**
   - Click on **"Surveys"** in the main navigation menu
   - **Expected Result**: You will see the Surveys List page

2. **Review available surveys**
   - The page displays all active surveys
   - Each survey card shows:
     - Survey title
     - Description
     - Start and end dates
     - Status indicator
     - "Take Survey" button (if not yet completed)
     - "Completed" badge (if already submitted)
   - **Expected Result**: You can see all surveys you're eligible to complete

3. **Identify survey status**
   - **Active surveys**: Available for completion (green indicator)
   - **Completed surveys**: You've already submitted responses (marked with a checkmark or "Completed" badge)
   - **Closed surveys**: No longer accepting responses
   - **Expected Result**: You can distinguish between available and completed surveys

### Task 2: Starting a Survey

Begin completing an available survey.

1. **Select a survey to complete**
   - From the Surveys List, find a survey you want to complete
   - Ensure it's marked as "Active" and not yet completed
   - **Expected Result**: You've identified a survey to take

2. **Click the "Take Survey" button**
   - Click the **"Take Survey"** or **"Start Survey"** button on the survey card
   - **Expected Result**: You are redirected to the survey form page

3. **Review survey information**
   - Read the survey title and description
   - Note the survey deadline (end date)
   - Review any instructions provided
   - **Expected Result**: You understand the survey purpose and requirements

### Task 3: Completing Survey Questions

Answer all survey questions according to their type.

#### Text Answer Questions

1. **Locate text input fields**
   - Text questions have a large text box for your response
   - **Expected Result**: Text input field is visible

2. **Enter your response**
   - Type your answer in the text box
   - Provide detailed responses as needed
   - **Expected Result**: Your text is entered in the field

#### Multiple Choice Questions (Single Answer)

1. **Review the options**
   - Multiple choice questions display radio buttons
   - You can select only one option
   - **Expected Result**: All options are visible

2. **Select your answer**
   - Click the radio button next to your chosen option
   - Only one option can be selected at a time
   - **Expected Result**: Your selected option is highlighted

#### Checkbox Questions (Multiple Answers)

1. **Review all available options**
   - Checkbox questions allow multiple selections
   - Each option has a checkbox
   - **Expected Result**: All options are visible with checkboxes

2. **Select all applicable answers**
   - Click the checkboxes for all options that apply
   - You can select multiple options
   - You can deselect by clicking again
   - **Expected Result**: All your selected options are checked

#### Rating Scale Questions

1. **Review the rating scale**
   - Rating questions show a scale (e.g., 1-5, 1-10)
   - The scale may represent different meanings:
     - 1-5: Poor to Excellent
     - 1-10: Low to High
     - Agreement scales: Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree
     - Satisfaction scales: Very Unsatisfied to Very Satisfied
   - **Expected Result**: You understand the rating scale

2. **Select your rating**
   - Click on the number or option that represents your rating
   - Some ratings may use stars, numbers, or buttons
   - **Expected Result**: Your rating is selected and highlighted

#### Likert Scale Questions

1. **Review the statement**
   - Likert questions present a statement for you to rate
   - Common scales include:
     - Agreement: Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree
     - Frequency: Never → Always
     - Satisfaction: Very Unsatisfied → Very Satisfied
   - **Expected Result**: You understand the statement and scale

2. **Select your response**
   - Choose the option that best represents your opinion
   - **Expected Result**: Your selection is highlighted

#### Date Questions

1. **Locate the date picker**
   - Date questions have a date input field
   - May include a calendar icon
   - **Expected Result**: Date input field is visible

2. **Enter or select a date**
   - Click the field to open a date picker, or
   - Type the date in the required format (usually YYYY-MM-DD)
   - **Expected Result**: Your selected date appears in the field

#### Time Questions

1. **Locate the time picker**
   - Time questions have a time input field
   - **Expected Result**: Time input field is visible

2. **Enter or select a time**
   - Click the field to open a time picker, or
   - Type the time in the required format
   - **Expected Result**: Your selected time appears in the field

#### File Upload Questions

1. **Locate the file upload button**
   - File upload questions have a "Choose File" or "Upload" button
   - **Expected Result**: Upload button is visible

2. **Select a file to upload**
   - Click the **"Choose File"** button
   - Browse your computer for the file
   - Select the file and click **"Open"**
   - **Expected Result**: The filename appears next to the upload button

3. **Verify the upload**
   - Ensure the correct file is selected
   - Check file size limits if displayed
   - **Expected Result**: File is ready for upload with submission

#### Email, Number, Phone, and URL Questions

1. **Locate the specialized input field**
   - These questions have formatted input fields
   - **Expected Result**: Input field is visible

2. **Enter your response**
   - Type your email, number, phone, or URL
   - The system may validate the format automatically
   - **Expected Result**: Your response is entered correctly

### Task 4: Handling Required Questions

Ensure all required questions are answered before submission.

1. **Identify required questions**
   - Required questions are marked with an asterisk (*) or "Required" label
   - These questions must be answered to submit the survey
   - **Expected Result**: You can identify which questions are mandatory

2. **Complete all required questions**
   - Answer every question marked as required
   - Leave optional questions blank if you prefer
   - **Expected Result**: All required questions have responses

3. **Check for validation errors**
   - If you try to submit without completing required questions:
     - The system will display error messages
     - Required fields will be highlighted
     - You'll be prompted to complete missing answers
   - **Expected Result**: You're notified of any missing required responses

### Task 5: Using Help Text

Utilize help text provided for questions that need clarification.

1. **Look for help text**
   - Some questions include help text below the question
   - Help text provides:
     - Additional context
     - Examples of good responses
     - Formatting guidelines
     - Clarifications
   - **Expected Result**: You can see help text when available

2. **Read and apply help text**
   - Review the help text before answering
   - Follow any guidelines or examples provided
   - **Expected Result**: You understand how to answer the question correctly

### Task 6: Reviewing Your Answers

Review all your responses before submitting the survey.

1. **Scroll through the survey**
   - Review each question and your answer
   - **Expected Result**: You can see all your responses

2. **Make changes if needed**
   - Edit any answers you want to change
   - Update responses before submission
   - **Expected Result**: Your changes are reflected in the form

3. **Verify required questions**
   - Ensure all required questions are answered
   - Check that your responses are complete
   - **Expected Result**: All required fields are filled

### Task 7: Submitting the Survey

Submit your completed survey responses.

1. **Locate the Submit button**
   - The **"Submit Survey"** button is typically at the bottom of the form
   - **Expected Result**: Submit button is visible

2. **Click the Submit button**
   - Click **"Submit Survey"** or **"Submit Responses"**
   - **Expected Result**: The system processes your submission

3. **Wait for confirmation**
   - The system validates your responses
   - You may see a loading indicator
   - **Expected Result**: Processing is in progress

4. **Receive confirmation**
   - After successful submission:
     - You'll see a success message: "Thank you for completing the survey!"
     - You're redirected to the Surveys List page
     - The survey is now marked as "Completed" in your list
   - **Expected Result**: You receive confirmation that your responses were saved

### Task 8: Accessing External Surveys

Complete surveys hosted on external platforms.

1. **Identify external surveys**
   - External surveys are marked with an "External Survey" badge or icon
   - They may show a link icon
   - **Expected Result**: You can identify external surveys

2. **Click the survey link**
   - Click **"Take Survey"** or **"Open External Survey"**
   - **Expected Result**: A new tab or window opens with the external survey

3. **Complete the external survey**
   - Follow the instructions on the external platform
   - Complete all required questions on that platform
   - Submit the survey according to the external platform's process
   - **Expected Result**: You complete the survey on the external site

4. **Return to the alumni system**
   - Close the external survey tab
   - Return to the alumni system
   - The system may automatically mark the survey as completed
   - **Expected Result**: You're back on the Surveys List page

### Task 9: Viewing Completed Surveys

Check which surveys you've already completed.

1. **Navigate to the Surveys List**
   - Go to the **Surveys** page
   - **Expected Result**: The surveys list is displayed

2. **Identify completed surveys**
   - Completed surveys show:
     - A "Completed" badge or checkmark
     - No "Take Survey" button
     - Submission date (if displayed)
   - **Expected Result**: You can see which surveys you've completed

3. **Understand completion restrictions**
   - You can only submit each survey once
   - Completed surveys cannot be edited or resubmitted
   - This prevents duplicate responses
   - **Expected Result**: You understand the one-time submission policy

## Tips and Best Practices

- **Read carefully**: Take time to read each question and all options before answering
- **Use help text**: If provided, help text can clarify what's expected in your response
- **Be honest**: Provide truthful responses to help administrators make informed decisions
- **Complete required questions**: Focus on required questions first, then optionals
- **Review before submitting**: Double-check your answers before clicking Submit
- **No time limit**: Take your time - surveys don't have time limits
- **One submission**: Remember you can only submit each survey once, so review carefully
- **Check deadlines**: Complete surveys before the end date to ensure your responses are counted
- **External surveys**: For external surveys, complete them promptly as the system may not track partial completion

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot Submit Survey
**Symptoms**: The Submit button doesn't work or shows an error
**Solution**:
- Ensure all required questions (marked with *) are answered
- Check for validation errors highlighted in red
- Verify that your responses are in the correct format (e.g., valid email, proper date format)
- Try refreshing the page and re-entering your responses
- Check your internet connection
- If the issue persists, contact system administrators

### Issue: Survey Already Completed Message
**Symptoms**: You see "You have already completed this survey" when trying to access a survey
**Solution**:
- This is expected behavior - each survey can only be submitted once
- Check the Surveys List to confirm you've already completed it
- If you believe this is an error, contact system administrators
- You cannot edit or resubmit completed surveys

### Issue: External Survey Link Not Working
**Symptoms**: Clicking an external survey link doesn't open the survey
**Solution**:
- Check if your browser is blocking pop-ups
- Try right-clicking the link and selecting "Open in new tab"
- Copy the survey URL and paste it in a new browser tab
- Ensure you have internet access
- Contact administrators if the external link is broken

### Issue: File Upload Fails
**Symptoms**: Cannot upload a file for a file upload question
**Solution**:
- Check the file size - it may exceed the maximum allowed size
- Verify the file format is accepted
- Try a different file or compress large files
- Check your internet connection
- Try using a different browser
- Contact support if the issue continues

### Issue: Survey Not Appearing in List
**Symptoms**: You expect to see a survey but it's not in your list
**Solution**:
- Verify the survey is in "Active" status (not Draft or Closed)
- Check if the survey has already ended (past end date)
- Ensure you're logged in with the correct account
- Refresh the page to load the latest surveys
- Contact administrators if you believe you should have access

### Issue: Responses Not Saving
**Symptoms**: Your answers disappear or don't save
**Solution**:
- Don't navigate away from the page before submitting
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Complete the survey in one session
- Click Submit only once and wait for confirmation
- If you must leave, you may need to start over (responses aren't auto-saved)

### Issue: Question Format Unclear
**Symptoms**: You don't understand what a question is asking
**Solution**:
- Read the help text if provided below the question
- Look for examples in the question description
- If still unclear, provide your best interpretation
- Contact administrators for clarification if needed
- You can also skip optional questions if you're unsure

## Additional Notes

- **Privacy**: Your survey responses are confidential and used for research and improvement purposes
- **IP Tracking**: Your IP address may be recorded for security and duplicate prevention
- **One-Time Submission**: Each survey can only be completed once per user
- **No Editing**: Once submitted, responses cannot be edited or deleted
- **Response Time**: There's no time limit for completing surveys
- **Partial Completion**: Responses are not saved until you click Submit
- **External Surveys**: External surveys may have different rules and features
- **Survey Results**: Some surveys may share aggregated results with participants
- **Mandatory Participation**: Some surveys may be required for certain benefits or programs

## Security and Privacy

- Your responses are stored securely in the database
- Only authorized administrators can view individual responses
- Aggregated data may be used for reports and analysis
- Your personal information is protected according to privacy policies
- External surveys are subject to the privacy policies of their hosting platforms

# Faq

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
Visit the registration page, fill in your email and password, complete the registration form, and verify your email address.

### I didn't receive the verification email. What should I do?
Check your spam folder. If you still don't see it, you can request a new verification email from the login page.

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, you can update your email address in your account settings. You'll need to verify the new email address.

## Profile Management

### How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to your profile page and click the edit button. You can update your personal information, education, work experience, and skills.

### Can I upload multiple documents?
Yes, you can upload transcripts, certificates, diplomas, and resumes. Each document type can have multiple files.

### How do I change my profile photo?
Go to your profile page, click on your current photo (or the placeholder), and upload a new image.

### Who can see my profile information?
By default, your profile is visible to other authenticated alumni users. You can adjust privacy settings in your account settings.

## Connections & Networking

### How do I connect with other alumni?
Browse the alumni directory, find the person you want to connect with, and send them a connection request.

### What happens when I send a connection request?
The recipient will receive a notification and can choose to accept or reject your request.

### Can I remove a connection?
Yes, you can remove connections from your connections list at any time.

## Messaging

### Who can I message?
You can send direct messages to your connections. You can also participate in group chats if you're a member.

### Can I send files through messages?
Yes, you can attach files to your messages.

### How do I create a group chat?
From the messaging interface, select "Create Group Chat," add participants from your connections, and start chatting.

## Events

### How do I RSVP to an event?
View the event details and click the RSVP button. You can choose to attend, not attend, or mark as maybe.

### Can I change my RSVP?
Yes, you can update your RSVP status at any time before the event.

### How do I find events I'm attending?
Navigate to the events page and filter by "My Events" or "Attending."

## Job Board

### How do I apply for a job?
View the job details, click "Apply," fill in the application form, and upload any required documents.

### Can I track my application status?
Yes, you can view all your applications and their current status in your job applications dashboard.

### What's the difference between internal and external jobs?
Internal jobs are posted by the alumni system and allow direct applications. External jobs link to third-party websites.

## Donations

### How do I make a donation?
Browse donation campaigns, select a campaign, enter your donation amount, and follow the payment instructions.

### What payment methods are accepted?
The system supports GCash and other payment methods. Upload your payment proof after completing the transaction.

### Can I see my donation history?
Yes, you can view all your past donations in your donation history.

## Alumni Groups

### How do I join a group?
Browse available groups, view group details, and click "Join Group." Some groups may require answering security questions.

### Can I create my own group?
Yes, authenticated users can create new alumni groups. Navigate to the groups section and click "Create Group."

### How do I leave a group?
Go to the group page and click "Leave Group."

## Admin Questions

### How do I access admin features?
Log in with your admin account and access the admin dashboard from the navigation menu.

### How do I create an announcement?
Navigate to Announcement Management, click "Create Announcement," fill in the details, and publish.

### How do I verify donation payments?
Go to Donation Management, view pending donations, review payment proofs, and update the status accordingly.

### Can I export user data?
Yes, admin users can export user data from the user management section.

## Technical Issues

### The page isn't loading. What should I do?
Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser.

### I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?
Check the [Troubleshooting Guide](troubleshooting.md) for common error messages and solutions.

### Who do I contact for technical support?
Contact the system administrators through the feedback form or the contact information provided on the site.

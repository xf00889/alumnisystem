# New User Guide

Welcome to the NORSU Alumni System! This guide will help you get started quickly and make the most of your alumni network.

## Overview

The NORSU Alumni System connects you with fellow alumni, provides career opportunities, and keeps you engaged with your alma mater. This guide walks you through the essential first steps.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### Registration Process

1. **Visit the registration page**
   - Navigate to the NORSU Alumni System homepage
   - Click the "Register" or "Sign Up" button

2. **Fill in your email and password**
   - Use a valid email address you check regularly
   - Create a strong password (at least 8 characters with letters and numbers)
   - Confirm your password

3. **Complete the registration form**
   - Enter your first name and last name
   - Provide your contact information
   - Select your graduation year and program
   - Fill in any other required fields

4. **Submit the form**
   - Review your information for accuracy
   - Click "Register" or "Create Account"

**Tip**: Use your personal email address rather than a work email, as work emails may change over time.

For detailed instructions, see [Registration Process](../public-features/registration.md).

## Step 2: Verify Your Email

### Email Verification

1. **Check your email inbox**
   - Look for an email from the NORSU Alumni System
   - Check your spam/junk folder if you don't see it within a few minutes

2. **Click the verification link**
   - Open the verification email
   - Click the verification link provided
   - You'll be redirected to the system

3. **Confirmation**
   - Your account is now activated
   - You can log in and start using the system

**Troubleshooting**: If you don't receive the email, you can request a new verification email from the login page.

For more details, see [Email Verification](../user-features/account-management/email-verification.md).

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

A complete profile helps you connect with other alumni and increases your visibility in the network.

### Essential Profile Information

1. **Log in to your account**
   - Use your registered email and password
   - Click "Login"

2. **Navigate to your profile**
   - Click on your name or profile icon
   - Select "My Profile" or "Edit Profile"

3. **Add personal information**
   - Update your contact details
   - Add your current location
   - Provide a brief bio or introduction
   - See [Personal Information](../user-features/profile-management/personal-information.md)

4. **Add education history**
   - Include your degree(s) from NORSU
   - Add graduation year and program
   - Mark your primary education
   - See [Education History](../user-features/profile-management/education-history.md)

5. **Add work experience**
   - List your current and past positions
   - Include company names and job titles
   - Add employment dates
   - See [Work Experience](../user-features/profile-management/work-experience.md)

6. **Add your skills**
   - List professional skills
   - Include technical and soft skills
   - Categorize by skill type
   - See [Skills Management](../user-features/profile-management/skills.md)

7. **Upload a profile photo**
   - Use a clear, professional photo
   - Ensure good lighting and quality
   - See [Profile Photo](../user-features/profile-management/profile-photo.md)

**Tip**: A complete profile with a photo gets more connection requests and engagement!

## Step 4: Explore Key Features

### Connect with Alumni

**Browse the Alumni Directory**
- Navigate to the Alumni Directory
- Use search and filters to find classmates, colleagues, or alumni in your field
- View alumni profiles to learn about their careers
- See [Browsing Directory](../user-features/alumni-directory/browsing.md)

**Send Connection Requests**
- Click "Connect" on alumni profiles
- Add a personal message (optional but recommended)
- Wait for them to accept your request
- See [Sending Requests](../user-features/connections/sending-requests.md)

**Build Your Network**
- Start with people you know from your time at NORSU
- Connect with alumni in your industry or location
- Expand your professional network
- See [Connections Overview](../user-features/connections/README.md)

### Stay Updated

**Check Announcements**
- View the latest news and updates
- Filter by category (academic, events, general)
- Stay informed about alumni activities
- See [Public Announcements](../public-features/announcements.md)

**Browse Upcoming Events**
- Discover alumni gatherings, reunions, and networking events
- View event details including date, time, and location
- See [Browsing Events](../user-features/events/browsing-events.md)

**RSVP to Events**
- Indicate your attendance for events
- Receive event reminders and updates
- Connect with other attendees
- See [Event Participation](../user-features/events/event-participation.md)

### Explore Opportunities

**Visit the Job Board**
- Browse career opportunities posted by alumni and employers
- Filter by job type, location, and industry
- Apply for positions that match your skills
- See [Browsing Jobs](../user-features/jobs/browsing-jobs.md)

**Look for Mentorship**
- Find experienced alumni who can guide your career
- Search for mentors in your field of interest
- Request mentorship and schedule meetings
- See [Mentor Search](../user-features/mentorship/mentor-search.md)

**Join Alumni Groups**
- Discover groups based on interests, location, or graduation year
- Join groups to connect with like-minded alumni
- Participate in group discussions and activities
- See [Browsing Groups](../user-features/groups/browsing-groups.md)

## Quick Tips for Success

1. **Complete Your Profile**: A detailed profile helps you get found and makes better connections
2. **Be Active**: Regularly check for announcements, events, and messages
3. **Connect Authentically**: Add personal messages when sending connection requests
4. **Give Back**: Consider mentoring younger alumni or contributing to campaigns
5. **Stay Professional**: Maintain a professional tone in all interactions
6. **Update Regularly**: Keep your profile current with new jobs, skills, and achievements
7. **Explore Features**: Take time to discover all the system has to offer

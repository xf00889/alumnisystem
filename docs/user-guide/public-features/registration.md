# Registration Process

## Overview

The Registration Process allows new users to create an account on the NORSU Alumni System. Registration is required to access personalized features, connect with other alumni, apply for jobs, RSVP to events, and participate in the alumni community.

## Who Can Use This Feature

- **All visitors** who want to create an account
- NORSU alumni and community members
- Anyone interested in joining the NORSU alumni network

## Prerequisites

- Valid email address
- Internet connection and web browser
- Access to your email for verification

## How to Access

1. Navigate to the NORSU Alumni System website
2. Click "Register" or "Sign Up" in the navigation menu
3. Or visit: `/accounts/signup/`

## Key Features

- Email-based registration
- Email verification for security
- Post-registration profile completion
- Secure password requirements
- Account activation process
- Profile setup wizard

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Task 1: Email Registration

1. Navigate to the registration page
   - Click "Register" or "Sign Up" in the main navigation
   - **Expected Result**: Registration form is displayed

2. Fill in the registration form
   - **Required fields**:
     - Email address (must be valid and unique)
     - Password (must meet security requirements)
     - Password confirmation (must match password)
   - **Expected Result**: Form fields are ready for input

3. Review password requirements
   - **Password must**:
     - Be at least 8 characters long
     - Contain at least one uppercase letter
     - Contain at least one lowercase letter
     - Contain at least one number
     - Not be too common or easily guessable
   - **Expected Result**: Password requirements are displayed

4. Enter your email address
   - Use a valid email you have access to
   - This will be your primary contact email
   - **Expected Result**: Email is validated for correct format

5. Create a strong password
   - Follow the password requirements
   - Confirm password by entering it again
   - **Expected Result**: Password strength indicator shows strength level

6. Complete any CAPTCHA verification (if present)
   - Check the "I'm not a robot" box
   - Complete any additional verification steps
   - **Expected Result**: CAPTCHA is verified

7. Review and accept terms of service (if required)
   - Read the terms and conditions
   - Check the acceptance box
   - **Expected Result**: Terms are acknowledged

8. Click "Sign Up" or "Register" button
   - **Expected Result**: Registration is submitted

9. Receive confirmation message
   - You'll see a message: "Verification email sent"
   - **Expected Result**: Success message is displayed

### Task 2: Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox
   - Look for an email from the NORSU Alumni System
   - Subject line: "Verify your email address" or similar
   - **Expected Result**: Verification email is received within a few minutes

2. If you don't see the email
   - Check your spam/junk folder
   - Wait a few minutes and refresh
   - Request a new verification email if needed
   - **Expected Result**: Email is found

3. Open the verification email
   - **Email contains**:
     - Welcome message
     - Verification link
     - Instructions
     - Link expiration time (usually 24-48 hours)
   - **Expected Result**: Email opens successfully

4. Click the verification link
   - Click the "Verify Email" button or link in the email
   - **Expected Result**: You're redirected to the verification confirmation page

5. Receive verification confirmation
   - You'll see a success message
   - Your email is now verified
   - **Expected Result**: "Email verified successfully" message is displayed

6. Proceed to login or profile completion
   - You may be automatically logged in
   - Or prompted to complete your profile
   - **Expected Result**: You're directed to the next step

### Task 3: Post-Registration Profile Completion

After email verification, you'll be prompted to complete your profile with essential information.

1. Access the profile completion form
   - You're automatically redirected after email verification
   - Or navigate to the post-registration page
   - **Expected Result**: Profile completion form is displayed

2. Fill in personal information
   - **Required fields**:
     - First name
     - Last name
     - Date of birth
     - Gender
     - Phone number
   - **Expected Result**: Personal information fields are filled

3. Enter your address information
   - **Fields include**:
     - Street address
     - City
     - Province/State
     - Country
     - Postal code
   - **Expected Result**: Address information is entered

4. Provide educational background
   - **Required fields**:
     - Course/Program graduated
     - Graduation year
     - Campus (if applicable)
     - College/Department
   - **Expected Result**: Educational information is recorded

5. Add current employment information (optional but recommended)
   - **Fields include**:
     - Present occupation/position
     - Company name
     - Industry
   - **Expected Result**: Employment information is entered

6. Set privacy preferences (if available)
   - Choose what information is visible to other users
   - Set profile visibility options
   - **Expected Result**: Privacy settings are configured

7. Review all entered information
   - Double-check all fields for accuracy
   - **Expected Result**: All information is correct

8. Click "Complete Registration" or "Submit" button
   - **Expected Result**: Profile is saved

9. Receive confirmation
   - You'll see a success message
   - You're redirected to your profile or home page
   - **Expected Result**: Registration is complete and you're logged in

### Task 4: First Login After Registration

1. Navigate to the login page (if not already logged in)
   - Click "Login" in the navigation
   - **Expected Result**: Login form is displayed

2. Enter your credentials
   - Email address (used during registration)
   - Password (created during registration)
   - **Expected Result**: Credentials are entered

3. Check "Remember Me" (optional)
   - Keeps you logged in on this device
   - **Expected Result**: Option is selected if desired

4. Click "Login" or "Sign In" button
   - **Expected Result**: You're logged into your account

5. Access your dashboard
   - You're redirected to the authenticated home page
   - **Expected Result**: You see your personalized dashboard

## Tips and Best Practices

- **Use a Valid Email**: Ensure you have access to the email address you register with
- **Create a Strong Password**: Use a unique, strong password for security
- **Save Your Password**: Store your password securely (use a password manager)
- **Complete Profile Promptly**: Fill in your profile information while it's fresh
- **Verify Email Quickly**: Verification links may expire after 24-48 hours
- **Check Spam Folder**: Verification emails sometimes go to spam
- **Use Accurate Information**: Provide correct details for better networking
- **Update Profile Later**: You can always update your profile information later

## Password Security Tips

- Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and special characters
- Avoid using personal information (birthdate, name, etc.)
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites
- Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords
- Change your password regularly
- Never share your password with anyone

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Email already registered
**Solution**:
- The email address is already in use
- Try logging in instead of registering
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature if you don't remember your password
- Contact support if you believe this is an error

### Issue: Verification email not received
**Solution**:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes and check again
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Request a new verification email
- Check if your email provider is blocking the emails
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Issue: Verification link expired
**Solution**:
- Request a new verification email
- Click the "Resend Verification Email" link
- Complete verification within the time limit (usually 24-48 hours)

### Issue: Verification link not working
**Solution**:
- Copy and paste the full URL into your browser
- Ensure you're clicking the most recent verification email
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Request a new verification email
- Try a different browser

### Issue: Password doesn't meet requirements
**Solution**:
- Review the password requirements displayed
- Ensure password is at least 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, and numbers
- Avoid common passwords
- Try a password generator

### Issue: Cannot complete profile
**Solution**:
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check for error messages on specific fields
- Verify date formats are correct
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Issue: CAPTCHA not working
**Solution**:
- Refresh the page and try again
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere

### Issue: Account created but cannot login
**Solution**:
- Verify your email address first
- Check that you're using the correct email and password
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Try the "Forgot Password" feature
- Contact support for assistance

## What Happens After Registration

Once you complete registration and profile setup:

1. **Access to Features**: You can now access all registered user features
2. **Profile Created**: Your profile is visible to other alumni (based on privacy settings)
3. **Networking**: You can connect with other alumni
4. **Events**: You can RSVP to events
5. **Jobs**: You can apply for internal job postings
6. **Messaging**: You can send messages to connections
7. **Groups**: You can join and participate in alumni groups
8. **Donations**: Your donations are tracked in your history

## Next Steps After Registration

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Add more details to your profile
   - Upload a profile photo
   - Add work experience
   - List your skills
   - Upload documents (resume, certificates)

2. **Connect with Alumni**: Start building your network
   - Search for classmates
   - Send connection requests
   - Join alumni groups

3. **Explore Features**: Discover what the system offers
   - Browse events and RSVP
   - Check out job opportunities
   - Read announcements
   - Explore alumni directory

4. **Update Privacy Settings**: Control your visibility
   - Set who can see your profile
   - Manage notification preferences
   - Configure privacy options

## Privacy and Security

- **Email Privacy**: Your email is kept private and not shared publicly
- **Password Security**: Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- **Profile Visibility**: You control what information is visible to others
- **Data Protection**: Your personal information is protected according to privacy policies
- **Account Security**: Enable two-factor authentication if available

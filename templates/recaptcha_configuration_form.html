Getting reCAPTCHA v3 Keys

How to get your reCAPTCHA v3 keys:
  1. Visit the Google reCAPTCHA Admin Console
  2. Click "Create" to add a new site
  3. Enter your site details:
    • Label: NORSU Alumni System
    • reCAPTCHA type: reCAPTCHA v3
    • Domains: Add your domain (e.g., localhost, 127.0.0.1 for development)
  4. Accept the Terms of Service and click "Submit"
  5. Copy the Site Key and Secret Key to the form above
Important Notes:
  • Score Threshold: 0.5 is recommended for most applications
  • Lower threshold (0.3): More permissive, may allow some bots
  • Higher threshold (0.7): More strict, may block legitimate users
  • Testing: Use the test button to verify your configuration
